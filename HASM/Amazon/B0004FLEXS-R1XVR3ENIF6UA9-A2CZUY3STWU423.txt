Title: Don't Buy!!
Content:
Black & Decker 12 cup programmable is no good.  The main issue is low brewing temperature, wasting coffee.  I've tried two, same result.  Don't buy, put your money into a Bunn or Cuisinart that brew at correct temperature, about 202F.
