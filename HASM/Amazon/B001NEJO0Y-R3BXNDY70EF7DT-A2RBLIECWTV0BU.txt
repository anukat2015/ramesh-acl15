Title: Awesome laptop!!!!
Content:
I have been using my laptop for two days now, and I love it. Before purchasing this one I had a Dell Inspiron that I paid double the price for and it doesn't even compare. It is fast and is handling every application that installed with easy. As others mentioned previously, it does attract alot of fingerprints but it is not really a issue for me. Just use the cloth that comes with it to wipe it down after use.
