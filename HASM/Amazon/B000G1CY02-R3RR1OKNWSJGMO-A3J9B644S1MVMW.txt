Title: It gives me a warm feeling all over
Content:
Consumer Reports recommended this particular heater. (I've always depended on the kindness of Consumer Reports.) This is where I found the best retail price, and I got quick delivery of an undamaged good-looking unit. It's still too early to make a good judgment about how economical it is, but I've noticed that the oil furnace doesn't start up nearly as often as last year...and that was my plan.
