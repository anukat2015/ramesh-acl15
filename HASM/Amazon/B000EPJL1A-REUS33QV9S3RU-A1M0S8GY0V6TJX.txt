Title: Love it
Content:
I love the size and the fact that it clips to my workout clothes.  I never found a comfortable way to deal with my older shuffle and the neck lanyard was often in the way.  If you just want something to load your music on  and you aren't one to search for a particular tune-- this is a great workout buddy.
