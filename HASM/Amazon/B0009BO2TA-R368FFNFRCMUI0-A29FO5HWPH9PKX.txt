Title: You get what you pay for...
Content:
The metal tube that connects to the hose, does not have ANY locking mechanism, so it often comes apart, VERY annoying. I now have it rubber band together. Also, the power is pretty weak, you would have to go over the same spot several times just to get the smaller surface stuff sucked away.
