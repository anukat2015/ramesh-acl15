Title: Totally fabulous
Content:
I love this heater. It is quiet. it is powerful, it is reliable, and the remote works great.  others have mentioned that it is a problem having the display on the top rather than the front, but that does not bother me at all.  I have been through a bunch of ceramic heaters and this one is by far my favorite.
