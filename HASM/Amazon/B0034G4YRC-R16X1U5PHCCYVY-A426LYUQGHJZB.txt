Title: Great computer for the price!
Content:
I looked all over the internet before I purchased this computer and found nothing better than this deal on the gateway website.  I purchased the computer from Amazon and had a flawless transaction.  The computer is really fast for using solid works CAD software and easily handles many spread sheets, word docs, and internet windows.  Not a gamer so not sure how it would work for that.  The screen is awesomely clear!
Very Pleased!
