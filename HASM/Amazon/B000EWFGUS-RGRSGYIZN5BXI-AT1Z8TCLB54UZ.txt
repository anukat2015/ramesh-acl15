Title: Love it!
Content:
I don't drink a lot of coffee but I was very tired of coffee pots that leaked. This coffeemaker has a pot that doesn't leak. It also is very easy to program and makes a great cup of coffee. Now when I actually want to have good coffee from a coffee maker that isn't sitting in a puddle because the coffee pot leaked when I poured it, I can!
