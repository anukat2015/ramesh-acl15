Title: bought for kids
Content:
I bought this MP3 player for my kids (ages 7 & 9) to listen to their music and audio books.  They really enjoy them.  They are easy for the kids to use and simple to sync songs and books to.  I would recomend this MP3 for anyone who wants an easy to use, portable MP3 player.
