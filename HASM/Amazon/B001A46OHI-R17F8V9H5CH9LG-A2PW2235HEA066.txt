Title: Holmes HFH416 heater
Content:
Best space heater ever!  We bought ours in 2000 and it still going strong.  Best hot air penetration into the space of any space heater we've owned.  We later bought a model# HFH2986, but it's heat was not as robust as the 416.
Highly recommended.
