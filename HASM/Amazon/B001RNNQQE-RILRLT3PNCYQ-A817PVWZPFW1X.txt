Title: Very good coffee Maker
Content:
Used this Coffee Maker for about 10 days now and very pleased with it. Tried it with three different coffee blends, and all came out as expected. The thermal carafe does a good job of keeping the coffee hot for 2-3 hours. Plenty of time to drink it.
