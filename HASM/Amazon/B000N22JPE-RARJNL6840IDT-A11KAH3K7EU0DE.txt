Title: Cuisinart Coffee Pot
Content:
Coffee is hot and stays hot for a long time.   Although it is a 12 cup pot, there is a 1-4 cup setting if you only need a few cups.  You can pour coffee before the cycle is through.  Shuts down automatically in set time.  Programmable features which I have not touched yet.  Not too big and bulky.
