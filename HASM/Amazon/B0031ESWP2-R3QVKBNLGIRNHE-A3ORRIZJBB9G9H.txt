Title: This computer rocks!
Content:
I love this computer.  I was looking for a new laptop to replace my ancient one.  I did a lot of research and found this to be a smoking good deal for the price.  The fact that every single fingerprint shows up is a little abnoxious but the finish is beautiful so it is worth the trade off of having to wipe it down all of the time.  The laptop is super fast and has excellent features.  This is definately the laptop to buy!!!
