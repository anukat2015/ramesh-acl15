Title: Pelonis disc ceramic furnace
Content:
Very satisfactory for area heating. I use in front of my sliding glass patio door which is on the north facing wall of my home. I have owned one of these disc furnaces since in the 1980s. My original furnace is working still. I ordered a second one to use in my bathroom.
I have also had good results using this furnace in my work shop and in my work van.
