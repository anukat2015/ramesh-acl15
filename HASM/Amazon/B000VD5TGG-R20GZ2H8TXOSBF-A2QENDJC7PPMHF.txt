Title: New Big Green Clean Machine better than ever!
Content:
We had one of the original Big Green Clean Machines for many, many years and used it regularly - loved it.  When ours finally breathed its last, I started shopping for a new one and found that the new incarnation offered way more than the original.  I ordered it - it arrived promptly and we immediately started to use it.  It is a great machine and does virtually everything - shampooing, vacuuming - does it all.  Much better than the old one.  Can't go wrong buying this machine.
