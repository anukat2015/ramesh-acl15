Title: Great value!
Content:
I bought these for each of my two systems.  I recognized this t-stat as being identical to the one (albeit under a different brand name) I used in a previous home for 8½ years within incident. (In fact, I bought the previous t-stat from a friend who is an AC supply wholesaler and paid about the same price in 2001!)  Quick shipping, easy installation, great functionality and very tough to beat for the price!
