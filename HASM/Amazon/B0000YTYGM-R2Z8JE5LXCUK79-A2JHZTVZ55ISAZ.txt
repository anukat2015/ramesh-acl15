Title: Cuisinart Thermal Coffee Maker
Content:
This is the second one of these thermal coffee makers purchased, because we love it.  It is easy to use, clean and program, and because the thermal carafe keeps the coffee hot for hours, I can refill our coffee cups without having to go back and forth to the kitchen.  We  just take it with us where we are having our coffee, which could be in the back patio or when having lunch on the front terrace.
