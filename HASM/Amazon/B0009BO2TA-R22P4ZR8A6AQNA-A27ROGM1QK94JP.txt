Title: Fits the bill perfectly and affordably
Content:
Last year, I had hard wood floors installed in my home, and I wanted a new canister cleaner to clean up the dust and such.  I shopped around quite a bit locally before I found this Hoover.
I've been completely satisfied with this cleaner.  It would be nice if it were bagless because the bags are hard to find unless I buy them online.
