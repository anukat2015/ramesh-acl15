Title: Fantastic Mp3 player!
Content:
I owned the 2 GB version and loved it, but needed a little more storage. I love the sound, the ease of use, the view screen- everything! I love how it clips onto my shirt while I'm working out or working around the house. This is a fantastic purchase, and I highly recommend it to everyone!
