Title: Vornado Vortex Heat
Content:
I just had to post after reading the reviews.  
I have had a Vornado Vortex Heater since 1994, I paid twice what this one costs and it has been excellent.  Whisper quiet just like the Vornado fans.  It has moved with us, been used up and downstairs, been dropped and mistreated.  It was recalled and rebuilt in December of 2004 and I would definately buy another!
