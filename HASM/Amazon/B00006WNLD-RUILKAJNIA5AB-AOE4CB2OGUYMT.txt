Title: Perfect for College Dorms
Content:
Bought for my daughter in college and she and her 4 roommates have used it daily for a year and a half without any problems. Each can have her favorite flavored coffee.  Best of all they don't have to worry about buying filters or worry about replacing a broken coffee pot.
