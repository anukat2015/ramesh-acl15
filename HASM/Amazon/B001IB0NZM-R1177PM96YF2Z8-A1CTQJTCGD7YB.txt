Title: Review of Euro-pro EP703 Shark Plus
Content:
I bought this vacuum so I could clean nearer the walls on my wall-to-wall carpet.  It works well for this.  The only thing I don't like is that the instructions were so dark that I had to put white-out on one of them to make sure I did not press the wrong button (I put white-out on the on button which is black not to get it mixed up with the button for the cord).  It cleans well and I really like it.
