Title: VH2 Vortex Heater
Content:
The two VH2 space heaters I own have done quite well taking the chill out of a room on even the coldest of days.  I'm still using model EH-1 heater which I purchased in winter, 2003.  It has been a solid performer.  The model, unfortunately, in no longer produced.
