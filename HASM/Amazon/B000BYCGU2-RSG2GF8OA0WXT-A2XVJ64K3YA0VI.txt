Title: Good Purchase!
Content:
After researching coffeemakers for weeks, I finally decided to buy the Capresso despite some negative reviews. I am very happy with it. The coffee is hot! It makes great tasting coffee and is easy to use (if you follow directions!). I also bought the Capressor Burr Grinder and it is fabulous!
