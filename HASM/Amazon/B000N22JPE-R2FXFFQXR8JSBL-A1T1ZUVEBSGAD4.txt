Title: Cuisinart Coffemaker (Red)
Content:
This product works as described.  It is easy to use, easy to program, and easy to clean.  This coffeemaker makes a pretty good cup of coffee.  For the price, it would be hard to beat.
I would say that if you need a coffeemaker that is Red, to match everything else in your kitchen, you can not go wrong with this one.
