Title: we love the coffeemaker
Content:
After trying many different coffee makers, we finally came across Cuisinarts's Coffee-on-Demand.  Compared to other machines that make a cup at a time, this was a reasonable alternative.  We love the ease of making the coffee, cleaning the machine and the heat retention of the coffee without it tasting burned.
