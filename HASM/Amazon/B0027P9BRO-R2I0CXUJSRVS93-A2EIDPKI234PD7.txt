Title: Great So Far
Content:
Purchased this for my wife to replace Toshiba.  My wife has really enjoyed using the computer and likes its features.  It even came with a free upgrade to Windows 7 which was easy to received by going online with Sony.  The proof is in the pudding on how long this laptop will work, since Toshibas only lasted 3 years before problems.  This unit was loaded so hope it lasts longer.
