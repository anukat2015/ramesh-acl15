Title: Keurig B60 Special Edition
Content:
After trying all the k cups that came with my brewer, I felt it necessary to state that I love this brewer and the convenience it gives me.  I have owned a Senseo for 2-3 years and had been quite happy with it until now.  Now I can never go back to pods.  If you do get this Keurig, be sure to go to their website and register your brewer.  You get will receive a coupon code for two free boxes of k cups when you register.
