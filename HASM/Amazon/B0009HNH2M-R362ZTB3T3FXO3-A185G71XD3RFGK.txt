Title: Like This Vacuum
Content:
All but one room in my house is hardwood floors, but I do have area rugs.  I didn't want to spend a lot on vacuum just for one room and a few area rugs.  The price was right and I've been happy with it.  Works well on carpet runners and area works and does pretty well on hardwood and ok on uneven stone tiling.
