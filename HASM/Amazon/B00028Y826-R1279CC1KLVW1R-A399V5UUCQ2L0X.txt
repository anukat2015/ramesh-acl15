Title: Good but Flawed
Content:
I bought two of these. It had a wonderful demo music "African Song". and the songs loaded well. but it developed the classic snag...that many other buyers have been facing. the MP3 player has stopped working. the voice recording is working fine and the player playes exellent when plugged into the computer but once you try and play it indipendently it "hangs". and has to be switched off by pulling out the battery. :(.
