Title: Cute BUT......
Content:
Purchased this as a fill in until I could get my vac system installed. It sort of works.. it may or may not pick up the dirt on the carpet. Seems to work ok if you are just using the wand. I thought that with the Hoover name this would be a nice little vac to have on hand. Don't waste your money!
