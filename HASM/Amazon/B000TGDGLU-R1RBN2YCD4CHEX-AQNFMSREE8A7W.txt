Title: oil filled radiator
Content:
I am very satisfied,I have had this radiator for 2 months, and it heats the room very well. I actually have to turn it down or the room gets too hot.I live in a 4 bedroom house with just my wife, so instead of heating the whole house, we put this in the bedroom. With the door closed, the room stays warm. The medium setting is enough for the 12X12 room.
