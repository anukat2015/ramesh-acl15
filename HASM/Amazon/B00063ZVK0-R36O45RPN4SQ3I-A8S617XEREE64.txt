Title: Best space heater I ever bought!
Content:
It's so easy to operate.... just one-finger touch buttons... and it's very, very quiet.  The fan blows enough heat to heat the room perfectly.  This fan is stronger than a normal space heater, which is good.  It's very light to move around, with an easy-to-read control panel. I highly recommend it.  Buy it and you won't be sorry.
