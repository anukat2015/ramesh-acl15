Title: Simply put......"The Best"
Content:
In my opinion, Farberware makes the best coffee perculators on the planet.  We have every size coffee perculator made by Farberware and wouldn't use any other kind.  If fact, we have two of each size, one of each for our home and the others in our motorhome.  Being from south Louisiana, my wife and I enjoy freshly brewed rich and "potent" coffee first thing in the morning.  Farberware does it right every time.  Before we tried a Farberware perculator, we used many other types.  None match the brew of Farberware.
