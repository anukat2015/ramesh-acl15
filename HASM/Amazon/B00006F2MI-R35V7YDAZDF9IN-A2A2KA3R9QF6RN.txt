Title: The best coffee maker in the world
Content:
I love this machine.  I received this as a present for Christmas and it is the best present I have ever received.  I can make coffee at 6 am and have a cup from the same pot at 5pm and it is still warm.  My entire family has bought one over the past two years and no problems from any of them.  I highly recommend it!
