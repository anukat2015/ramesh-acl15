Title: Piece of junk...
Content:
I bought this item for my office about a year ago.  From day one, the fan made a terrible clicking sound unless the heater was propped up from the back to put the fan in a different rotary plane.  Then, as I turned the thing on this Fall....nothing.  No fan, no heat, no control panel lights...nothing at all.  It's headed for the dump.
