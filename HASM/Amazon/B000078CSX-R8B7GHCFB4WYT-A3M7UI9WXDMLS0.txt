Title: We love our Bunn-O-Matic
Content:
I purchased this for our veterinary hospital. I and others were spending lots of money at area coffee shops and I thought we could save money, gas and time if we had our own coffee maker. We love it. We also use it for when we have meetings and seminars at our place of business. I would recommend it highly! Very easy to use. Now the cofee shop experience is a present to myself on days where I can use a brief "vacation".
