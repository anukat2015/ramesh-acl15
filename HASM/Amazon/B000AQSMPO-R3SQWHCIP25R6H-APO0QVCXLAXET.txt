Title: AWESOME PRODUCT
Content:
I did a lot of research when purchasing a new tea maker - believe me it's hard to find a product specific for tea.  I have had two Mrs. Tea machines that were pretty good to me but it was time for something more.  The Keurig B60 is just awesome. I don't have a single complaint and could not be more happy for purchasing this product. I have ordered K-cups from both Amazon and Keurig directly and other than my increased consumption of tea, I could not be a happier customer!
