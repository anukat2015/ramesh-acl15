Title: Very nice heater for the price
Content:
I have been using this heater for a few weeks now and has worked very well so far.  The controls are easy to learn and use, especially the timer.  At the highest setting it really puts out the warmth and easily heats my small bedroom and hallway(175sq ft).  After seeing how much my October gas bill was I may purchase another heater and use them exclusively.
