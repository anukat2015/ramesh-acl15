Title: Great laptop-Light, Fast,Economical
Content:
This is the best computer that I have owned in recent years based on performance.  It is fast, can do more than one thing at a time, is lightweight and has very good battery life.  So far, I haven't found an undesireable thing about it.  I should have such good performance from my two year old desktop.  If I look happy, I am.
