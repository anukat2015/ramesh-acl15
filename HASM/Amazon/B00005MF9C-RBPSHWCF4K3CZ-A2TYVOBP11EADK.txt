Title: Great Product
Content:
My wife uses Decaf coffee and I enjoy the real stuff.  I was using instant coffee until I saw this item and ordered it immediately.  It is perfect for the morning coffee and a quick cup anytime.  Why pay a fortune for those one cup coffee makers and requisite supplies?  This does the job and it's easy to clean, store and use.
Black and Decker - you did it again!! By the way, it makes a great cup or two of coffee!
