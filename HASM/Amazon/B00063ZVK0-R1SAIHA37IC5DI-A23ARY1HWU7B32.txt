Title: Seems to Work Pretty Good
Content:
The heating is excellent in a normal sized room, probably won't do too much for a living room. Ups and Downs its fan speed and wattage to control room temperature perfectly.  I like it a lot, but after about a month is seems to be making a little fan noise off and on.
