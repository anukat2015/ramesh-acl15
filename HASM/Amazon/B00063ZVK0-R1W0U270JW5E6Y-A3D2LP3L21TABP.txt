Title: Not worth the money
Content:
I was very disappointed in this item.  It cost close to $100.00 and I have a $29.00 heater that heats better, faster and the fan moves the heat in a greater area.  I guess I was expecting a heater that was heavier and had more options.  I have learned my lesson, when I purchase an item that costs this much I will go to a local store and compare first or not order on line.  I would say that this heater was comparable to one that costs around $40.00 when I compared it after it arrived.
