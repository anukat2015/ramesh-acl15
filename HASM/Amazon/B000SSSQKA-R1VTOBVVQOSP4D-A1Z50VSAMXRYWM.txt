Title: Looking to Execute a Vacuum Cleaner? Try this.
Content:
This vacuum cleaner has so little suction that cleaning my area rug poorly takes at least 45 minutes. The brush gets so clogged so quickly that all I do is rip stuff out of it after a few minutes. It is so bloodly awful that there is no word bad enough for it.
