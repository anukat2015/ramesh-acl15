Title: Super vacuum for hard floors!
Content:
We just got rid of all our carpets and put laminate and ceramic flooring. Most vacuum cleaners have a power brush which is useless on a hard floor surface. The Dirt Devil is perfect for our purposes. It is light, fairly quiet and the hepa filter is super. Recommend this product.
