Title: Impossibly Slow; but beautiful....
Content:
I bought the green HDD model on an impulse but returned it.  It is impossibly slow.  Downgrading to Windows XP may not be impossible, but it's not easy, either.  For this price, you really have to be in love with the form rather than the function.  Sorry, I wish it were otherwise.
