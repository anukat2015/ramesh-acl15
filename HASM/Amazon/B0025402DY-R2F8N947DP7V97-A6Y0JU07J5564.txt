Title: Great Little Player
Content:
I love this little player. It is so easy to use and put music on. Anyone that loves music should have one of these. To charge it you just plug into your computer which I find to be very handy. Would recommend this player to anyone. The sound is great although I did buy a different earplug because the ones that came with the player hurt my ears. They to had a great sound.
