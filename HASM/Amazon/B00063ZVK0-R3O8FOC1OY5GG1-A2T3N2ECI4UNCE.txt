Title: Vornado Heater as Good As Their Fans
Content:
We have 3 space heaters in the house and this one is superior.  It is quiet and heats our 15X15 room nicely. Our other heaters just keep us warm if we stand right by them. I use it as an adjunct to our gas furnace for a room that doesn't get as warm as other rooms in the house. We have used this for almost 2 months and have absolutely no complaints.
