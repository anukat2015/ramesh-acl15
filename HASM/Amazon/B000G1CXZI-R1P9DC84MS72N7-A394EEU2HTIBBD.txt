Title: Great Quiet efficient little heater
Content:
I bought this heater to heat one room, my office which is 10'x12'. This allows me to leave rest of house heat off during the day.  This is an efficient quiet little heater. I can set a timer and regulate temp. Because it heats the oil in the drum, it stays warm for quite a while even when it is turned off. I intend to buy another one!
