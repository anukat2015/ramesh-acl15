Title: WindChaser PARHAL66 Halogen Far Infrared Parabolic Heater
Content:
I purchased one as a gift and one for my own home. We both are amazed at the amount and pleasantness of the heat output from this heater.  This heat concept was new to both of us, and I did not want to sink large amounts of money into something so unknown.  We are so pleased that we are considering larger units for our homes.  And this review comes during one of the coldest weeks we have experienced in years!
