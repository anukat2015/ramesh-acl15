Title: Kozy World Heater
Content:
Heater works well in heating the living space with much less natural gas than using the forced air heater for the home.  Initially the heater emitted a strong odor that permated the living space. It was probably the paint and metal parts burning off their new coating. It eventually disappeared and continues to heat well. I recommend this product.
