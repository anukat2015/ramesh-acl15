Title: Excellent Compact Heater
Content:
An excellent compact heater.  Easily maintains a 24 ft travel trailer comfortable in 30 deg weather.  Provides a steady, constant source of heat.  Have not yet used the programable feature, but judge it to be a plus.  Fin surface does get uncomfortably hot to the touch so should be used with caution around small children or pets.
