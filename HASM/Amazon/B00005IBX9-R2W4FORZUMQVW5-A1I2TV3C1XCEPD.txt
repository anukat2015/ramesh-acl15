Title: First Class Coffee Machine
Content:
Coffee is our first priority every morning. We've gone through three well known brands in the past 12 months because of various deficiencies. The DCC 1200 has everything we need in a coffee maker, great 12 cup capacity, easy to use control panel, reliable heating mechanism keeps the coffee very hot, efficient and delicious 4 cup brewing also, handsome design .We use paper with the permanent filter, for a smoother flavor.
