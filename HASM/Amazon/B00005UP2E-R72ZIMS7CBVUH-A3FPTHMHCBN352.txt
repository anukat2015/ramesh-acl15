Title: Dirt Devil 082500 Breeze Bagless Canister Vacuum
Content:
Terrifically efficient product for lifting dust and dust bunnies off hardwood floor and other rugless floors.  Works well on light rugs too.  Have not used on denser rugs.  Attachments are easy to put on and remove.  Light weight with long cord.  Easy to store.  Highly recommended for small apartments.
