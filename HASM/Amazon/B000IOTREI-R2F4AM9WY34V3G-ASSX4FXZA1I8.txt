Title: Next best thing to sitting in front of the fire place!
Content:
Seriously if you do not have a fire place this really is the next best thing. When you turn it on it heats up instantly. I live in an apartment and I rarely turn the heat on because I have the Presto. It's perfect for small spaces or when you need feel that instant warmth!
