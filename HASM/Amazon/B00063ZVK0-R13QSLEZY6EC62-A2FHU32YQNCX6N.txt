Title: Exactly waht I was looking for.
Content:
I wanted this for my baby's room and after going through 2 other heaters I was afraid I was never going to find the right one.  Well- I'm glad I finally ordered this one.  The heater is safe (with the auto shut off) and quiet.  Also- it provides gentle, effective heat throughout the entire room.
I think its a tiny bit pricey but its worth every penny.
