Title: Great Little Vacuum
Content:
A good vacuum for floors with out a carpet. a lot of power and is good for small type of rugs and bare floors. I got this one because of a wood floors and tiles. And if you want a for carpet then get a vacuum for carpet and stay away from not this one. 
  
 Cord could be a little longer but is OK just keep unplug in. Still 5 stars..............
