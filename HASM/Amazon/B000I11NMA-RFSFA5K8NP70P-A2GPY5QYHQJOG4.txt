Title: Holmes Quartz Heater
Content:
This is an excellent product.  We wanted it for our motorhome so we didn't have to use the propane heater all the time when we're hooked up to electricity.  We had tried 2 other heaters and returned them because they didn't stay on, didn't maintain the temperature and didn't seem to have the power.   This one is perfect.  It keepes the motorhome at the temperature set with no problems.  It is wonderful.
