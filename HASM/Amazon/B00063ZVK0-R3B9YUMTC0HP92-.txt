Title: Finally a heater that works
Content:
Bought this heater when the temperatures went to -40 outside to replace old base board type heater. The difference was incredible, although both units are 1500 watts the Vornado was much more effecient at distributing the heat across the room. Probably will purchase another when our other base board heater goes kaput
