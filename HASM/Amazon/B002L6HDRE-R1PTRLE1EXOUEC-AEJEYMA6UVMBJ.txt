Title: Another winner from Apple
Content:
This is an outstanding new ipod from Apple.  I have had 3 previous models and this one exceeds them all.  The FM radio is excellent with superb sound and sensitivity...stations come in clearly.  The pedometer is a welcome plus and the video camera is a Wow.  The finish has improved with vibrant colors and the whole ipod is very compact and more elegant than prior models.  I can hardly wait for the next generation.
