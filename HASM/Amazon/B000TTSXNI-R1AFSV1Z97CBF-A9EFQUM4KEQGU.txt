Title: Excellent Product..
Content:
I bought this heater to replace one that used up to much electricity. I love the fact that the auto thermostat keeps it comfortable in my living room. The auto timer is also convenient when you don't always remember to shut it off before leaving the house...I would definitely recommend this as an inexpensive but great heating source.
