Title: RCA S2501 Jet Stream MP3 1GB with Wireless Headphones
Content:
I bought this MP3 specifically for its wireless headphones.  The wireless headphones are great & I use them regularly without any problems or comfort issues with the over-ear attachments for road running & in the gym.  The MP3 controls are very easy to use & the colored screen very clear to read.  The only downside is only 1GB of storage but hopefully RCA will make a bigger one with wireless headphones soon. Absolutely LOVE this MP3!!!
