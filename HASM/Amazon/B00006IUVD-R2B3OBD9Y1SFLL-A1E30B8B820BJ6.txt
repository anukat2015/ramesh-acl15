Title: Good cup o'joe
Content:
This is perfect for a quick cup of coffee or tea.  It is simple to use and gets a hot cup of coffee or tea in your hands in about 30 seconds. The travel mug fits neatly in the car cup holder.  I am very happy with the coffee maker.  Best of all, it is small and does not take up a lot of space on your kitchen counter.
