Title: Great Coffee
Content:
This brewer makes great coffee.  It brews quickly, and it is amazingly easy to clean up.  Just pull out the used K-Cup and throw it away.  The only downside at all is that the K-Cups are somewhat more expensive than buying generic coffee beans.
