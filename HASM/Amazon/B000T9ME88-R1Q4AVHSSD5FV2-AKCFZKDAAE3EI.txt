Title: Sansa Shaker
Content:
Excellent mp3, except for one thing; the cover for the battery / SD card comes off every time you advance the songs.  Fortunately there is a strap to keep the cover attached to the player, so it won't get lost.  Otherwise it's an excellent purchase.
