Title: Perfect Perfect Perfect
Content:
I was looking for a 4 cup auto shut off coffee maker. The added programmable feature is an extra bonus.
When the coffee is done brewing it is VERY hot, which I love. The auto shut off works and being able to set the time to start brewing wasnt something I was looking for, but cant live without now.
I have had this coffee maker for about about 2 months and have absolutely no complaints at all about this product.
