Title: DeLonghi HHHP 1500 Mica Heater
Content:
We like the quiet, effective heat the DeLonghi Mica Heater produces.  The heater is light weight and easy to move, though the heat radiating from the back would prevent us from feeling comfortable about hanging the unit on a wall.
