Title: Not for Cat Litter
Content:
I bought this needing something to pick up the cat litter on my kitchen and bathroom floors. It's a big job with 5 cats and I thought it would make my life easier. It does NOT pick up the cat litter nor does it pick up much else. I gave it to a friend with no pets.
