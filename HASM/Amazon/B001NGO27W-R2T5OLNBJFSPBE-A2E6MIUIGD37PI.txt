Title: Excellent Coffee Maker!!!
Content:
I just gave this Cusinart dcc-2600 coffee maker to my hubby for an early Christmas gift--we both are loving it!!!  We've held off for way too to purchase a new coffee maker, just to be cost conscientious, but my hubby (the coffee connoisseur) has been making remarks about how great the coffee tastes during visits with family & friends--so I did my research and decided to buy this machine.  Wow!!!  Now, we are enjoying delicious coffee in our own home!...Looks like our old Proctor-Silex coffee maker will be in full retirement now!
