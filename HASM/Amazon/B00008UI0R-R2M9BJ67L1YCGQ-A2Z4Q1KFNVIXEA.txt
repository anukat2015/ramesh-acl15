Title: Best vacuum ever
Content:
I have purchased several upright vacuums over the past few years and decided to go with a cannister for my hard floors.  I was reluctant at first because I thought it wouldnt be as powerful as an upright - wrong! This vacuum has as much suction power if not more.  I recommend this particular model to anyone considering one of those fancy, expensive vacuums being advertised.
