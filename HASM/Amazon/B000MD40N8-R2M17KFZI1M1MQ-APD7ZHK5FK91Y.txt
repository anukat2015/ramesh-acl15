Title: Great for the Price
Content:
The Sandisk is a great MP3 player for the price.  It has all the features for which I was looking.  Its sleek design is cool!  The earbuds are lame and fall out of your ears.  Also, it is not the most intuitive for the non-savvy.  Overall it is pretty awesome!
