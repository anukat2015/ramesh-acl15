Title: Great space heater
Content:
We returned a heater with a fan because it made so much noise and got this one instead. It's very quiet, the heat output is adjustable to whatever temp you consider perfect, and, even though it's fairly large, is pretty lightweight and has a carrying handle. The cats and I love it!
