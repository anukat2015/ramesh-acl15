Title: Miele Neptune
Content:
This was a gift for my daughter.  She loves it.  She is happy with the quality and the performance.  She's had mainly Kenmore products and says this machine is powerful and easy to maneuver.  Her only complaint was that the tools are on a separate carrier that she has to move around with her, versus mounted on the vacuum cleaner itself.
