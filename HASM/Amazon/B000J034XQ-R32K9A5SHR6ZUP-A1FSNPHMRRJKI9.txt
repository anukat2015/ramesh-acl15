Title: BUY IT!
Content:
BUY  IT!!!
My wife picked this out and I have never been impressed with the electric brewers of our past. I am a nit picking pain when it comes to appliances.I was very surprised how well this thing works and makes perfect coffee each time (assuming you put in reasonable coffee) (I'm drinking a cup right now) We have had it about 1 year and if it broke tomorrow I would buy another one. 
One caveat!!! As a result, I drink a lot more coffee now.
Good Luck
