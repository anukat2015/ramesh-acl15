Title: Classy product
Content:
This is the second Vaio I have purchased, the first was for myself and the second for my son at college. Having had mine there was really no doubt, the laptop is well made and stylish, easy to use and basically a quality product. Only negative is battery life which is probably not as good as some of the competition.
