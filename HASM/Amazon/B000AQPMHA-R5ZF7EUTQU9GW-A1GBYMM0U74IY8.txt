Title: Nice for the price.
Content:
This is a nice machine for those on a budget, it makes great coffee one cup at a time, you can get a large variety of coffee flavors for this machine and if your like me, a person who loves coffee but doesn't have the time or desire to drink a whole pot it's perfect. Easy to use and easy to clean.
