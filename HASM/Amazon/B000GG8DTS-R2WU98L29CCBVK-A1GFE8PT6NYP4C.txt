Title: Awesome little dish
Content:
I have used this product on countless cold mornings. The instant focused heat, good for one or two persons, is a joy. Beats the inefficient and noisy forced air central heating. I wanted to buy a second dish, unfortunately it had gone out of stock. I will eagerly look for it again next season.
