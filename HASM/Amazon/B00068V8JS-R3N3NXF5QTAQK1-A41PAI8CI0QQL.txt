Title: It's a joke
Content:
Set at the highest temperature, this thing couldn't heat a small bathroom.  Waste of money.  My cats and dog love to find a warm spot in the house.  They can find the smallest ray of sunshine on the floor.  They think this heater is just another piece of furniture.  I was tricked by the high-tech sounding "Micathermic Heater".  What a JOKE!!!
