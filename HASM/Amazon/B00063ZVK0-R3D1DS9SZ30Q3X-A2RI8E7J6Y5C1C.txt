Title: Ok for small room
Content:
It didn't work well on my living room which is quite large, but warms up pretty well in my bedroom if it keep all the doors closed. It takes a little while to warm up even on my bedroom.
For small rooms its okay to use, I won't recommend this for larger room, Also it does not come with remote for this price.
