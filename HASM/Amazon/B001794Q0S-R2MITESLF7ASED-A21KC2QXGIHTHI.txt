Title: Great on-the-go
Content:
Makes the commute almost bearable. Unbreakable, easy to use, light, and ergonomic on  the commuter train. However, fill the cup in the sink to avoid the assembled unit tipping over and be sure to clean the cup and funnel thoroughly EVERY time you use them.
