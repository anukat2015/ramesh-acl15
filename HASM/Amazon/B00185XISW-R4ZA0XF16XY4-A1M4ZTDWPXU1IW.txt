Title: So far so good!
Content:
We use this in our kids room. Works great, we put it up high on the wall so they can't reach it to get burned or turn on and off. Helps heat the room up nicely without over heating. Takes the cold edge off the room that heating the house alone doesn't do. Also didn't hike up the electric bill.
