Title: Love it!
Content:
This little pot is perfect for 2-4 cups. I tried two other brands of pots and was not happy with them, the Krups looked like a thermal carafe but was only stainless looking, and you can't see inside the pot as to when it is done or how much is left,  tried Hamilton Beach, not hot enough. Love this little pot! Don't even bother with the other brands just get this one first.
