Title: Great computer
Content:
This laptop was just what we were looking for: easy to use, starts up lighting fast, and can multi-task several programs at the same time.  Because it contains the newer DDR3 RAM, which has twice the channels of DDR2, it should provide us with more years of use.
 
I was told by several people that Toshiba has a great reputation for quality, and we are very pleased with this computer.
