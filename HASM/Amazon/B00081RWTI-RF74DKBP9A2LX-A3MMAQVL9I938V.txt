Title: A great cup of coffee
Content:
There is nothing like waking up to a freshly brewed cup of coffee made from freshly ground beans. This coffee maker is easy to use, brews great coffee (based on the quality of the beans you purchase...bad beans do not make good coffee), and keeps the coffee hot for a long time.
