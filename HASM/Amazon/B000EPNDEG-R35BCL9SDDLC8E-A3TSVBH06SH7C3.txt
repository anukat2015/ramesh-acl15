Title: iPod but no accessories
Content:
Because we are parents - buying for a teen - and are not users of iPods ourselves, we aren't particularly knowlegeable inthis area, but we were very surprised to get the iPod and realize it came without any of the accessories.  It seems reasonable to assume that it would come with the earpieces so that you could actually listen to it!
