Title: WOW A+++++
Content:
What a great vacuum.  I used it just once on my bedroom floor and couldn't believe the amount of dirt that my old vacuum had been missing.  The small head works great on upholstery and in small places.  Warning:  The first few times you use this, you will have to dump the collection basket after each room -- because you will be getting dirt out that you didn't even know was there!!!
