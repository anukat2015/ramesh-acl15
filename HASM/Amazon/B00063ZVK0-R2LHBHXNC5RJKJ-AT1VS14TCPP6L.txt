Title: Good Heater!
Content:
Good heater! I bought this unit because a previous model was damaged and rendered inoperable. This model keeps my room nice and toasty, especially during the cold winter months. I usually keep the door closed for maximum effect. The LED temperature control is an excellent addition and very useful feature.
