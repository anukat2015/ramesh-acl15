Title: Great DSLR
Content:
This is a well designed, and well made product.  Since this is my first digital camera, I was prepared to be frequently baffled and frustrated by a steep learning curve.  Not the case--  for having as many controls as it does, operating it is very intuitive and the results are impressive.
