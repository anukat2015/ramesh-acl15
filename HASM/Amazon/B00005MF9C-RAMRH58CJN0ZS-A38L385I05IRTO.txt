Title: Brew 'n Go
Content:
I like this product so much that this purchase is a replacement for a unit I've had for about 10 years. It hasn't given up yet, but I thought I'd get a backup since they no longer seem to be available in stores. I'm a coffee drinker and my wife isn't. During the week it just is not practical to brew a pot of coffee just for me since I'll only be drinking one cup before I go to work. The Brew 'n Go is the perfect solution. I love it!
