Title: Very Satisfied
Content:
I received this item at least a week before they told me I should expect it. I have only had it a little over a month but am very satisfied with it. It is simple to use, the coffee is ready in minutes and simple to wash out the few parts after each use. I have not done a full cleaning yet but don't expect that to be a problem as it just requires you to run a regular cycle.
