Title: Not Reliable
Content:
Initially, we loved it!  However, less than one after we bought it, it stopped brewing hot coffee. Puzzled, I thought I would check out the reviews of this product while shopping for a new coffee maker.  I was shocked to read that this happened to someone else as well. I guess I should have done more homework before making my purchase!
