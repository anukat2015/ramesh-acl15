Title: Apple IPOD Touch 2nd Generation
Content:
Great Product sold by Amazon with a cost savings.  This is by far the greatest MP Player on the market.  Does everything from playing music, viewing video's to surfing the web.  Only drawback is the slide show for pic does not have random transition vs the classic version has random transition which makes a great slide show presentation.  The battery life is not long, but you can always purchase an exterior battery to extend playing time.  Other than that, everthing else is really great.
