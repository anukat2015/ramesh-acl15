Title: De Longhi Oil filled heater
Content:
This is an excellent oil heater!!  I ordered 3 in total.  Using two of them to heat up a drawing room with large bay windows which has no central heating due to serious pipe damage - which cannot be repaired at this time.
In 14F temperature outside the two of them were able to maintain a comfortable "sweet' temperature in the room and we spend a lot of time in there every evening!    Highly recommended for delicate humans like us who have sensitivity to cold in houses!
