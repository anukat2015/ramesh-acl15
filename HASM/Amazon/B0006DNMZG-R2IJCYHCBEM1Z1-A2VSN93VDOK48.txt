Title: Noise and poorly made
Content:
If you're a hard sleeper, then this may be ok.  But if like most folks the noisy fan will wake you up all night.  The fan rubs against the internal frame is is poorly designed.  The thermostat is so sensitive it clicks on and off every few seconds.  Keep looking...
