Title: Love this player!
Content:
The Pink Zune is my first mp3 player. 
After exhaustive research and listening to all the top mp3 players, I chose the Zune.  I read quite a lot of bad reviews on the Zune I have not regretted my choice once.  
This player is easy to use, the sound quality is amazing and the videos are breathtaking.  
I highly recommend the Zune Premium earbuds as well.
Buy the Zune, you won't be disappointed.
