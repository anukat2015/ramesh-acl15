Title: Home Hearth Electric Heater
Content:
Best investment I've ever made.  Just what was needed to take the "chill" out of the air on those cold winter days/nights.  Plus, the ambiance is great.  Looks very close to a real wood burning stove with out the mess.  Now, if they could only figure out a way to make it smell like real wood!
