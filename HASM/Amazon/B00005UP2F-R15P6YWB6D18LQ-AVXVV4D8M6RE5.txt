Title: 2 Dogs, No more hair all over.
Content:
I live in a small apartment on the 2 floor of an apartment building in Manhattan with 2 dogs and a girlfriend. Needless to say it gets dusty and hairy. For years I had a layer of hair on the carpet that my vacuum couldn't get rid of.When I busted out my new Dirt Devil canister vacuum the difference was amazing. It has awesome power and is fairly quiet. Additionally the size is a lot easier for a small place than my upright was.
