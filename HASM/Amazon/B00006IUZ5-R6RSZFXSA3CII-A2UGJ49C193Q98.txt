Title: Sunbeam Hot Shot
Content:
I first purchased this item several years ago and was so impressed by the way it worked and that there were no problems at all with it.  So when I was looking for a new machine I again turned to Sunbeam and was very happy to find the same (although a little updated) product available and at a terrific price.  I would not hesitate to recommend this product to anyone.
