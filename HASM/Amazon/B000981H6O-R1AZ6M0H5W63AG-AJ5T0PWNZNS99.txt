Title: Handy machine
Content:
This small vacuum gets used more than any bigger one I have ever owned.  It hangs on the wall in my pantry and is used in every room in the house.  It is used to clean everything from dirt at doorways to spilled coffee on the counter.  It is the handiest gadget I own.  We bought a second one for our camper to vacuum the floor when we are not connected to electricity.  Handy dandy machine.!!!!
