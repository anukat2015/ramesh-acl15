Title: Spectacular Product
Content:
I purchased this 5G Nano to replace my 2G.  Simply stated, it is a fantastic product.  Everything I liked about the 2G Nano has been improved upon and the new features are just as excellent.  I particularly like the FM radio.  If there is something that could be done better, you have to look to someone else for particulars.  I think this Nano is perfect.
