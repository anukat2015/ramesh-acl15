Title: I love this..It is perfect for me!
Content:
I had stopped drinking coffee at home because I only drink a couple of cups a day and I hated wasting any coffee.  I never waste any coffee with this CoffeeToGo.  I make one of the travel cups full, and that is exactly what I like to drink.  And it is the perfect temperature for me, too.  I never have liked burning my tongue when I try to enjoy my coffee and that doesn't happen with this little coffee maker.  Thanks, Cuisinart, for this new item.  I love it!!!
