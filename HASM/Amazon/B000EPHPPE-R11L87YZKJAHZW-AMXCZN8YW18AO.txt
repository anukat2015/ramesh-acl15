Title: I Love ipods
Content:
Apart from a minor panic when I didn't have the latest update on my computer all went extremely well. I have the whole of my collection on here now and have backed up at home so I hopefully won't lose anything. I love being able to run through all of them as I choose different albums to those favourites I always pick up. Haven't yet tried downloading any films but guess that's the next step. Highly recommended
