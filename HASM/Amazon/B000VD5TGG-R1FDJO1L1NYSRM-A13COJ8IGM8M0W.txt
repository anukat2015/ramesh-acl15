Title: Good vacuum, great carpet cleaner.
Content:
The other reviews of this product were an excellent guide of what to expect.  This is not the best vacuum in the world, but I think it's an excellent carpet cleaner.  There are lots of little bits and pieces to learn, but the owner's manual answered all my questions.
