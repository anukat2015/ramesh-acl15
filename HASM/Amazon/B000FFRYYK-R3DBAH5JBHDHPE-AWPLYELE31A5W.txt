Title: Bunn Thermal Carafe Coffee Brewer
Content:
As far as I'm concerned, the best coffee pot I've ever used.  I've used Bunn for years--this is my second one with the thermal carafe--and don't know of another coffee maker I would like well enough to buy.  Fresh, great quality coffee in three minutes--who neeeds to set a timer the night before?  Put the thermal carafe on your table, and enjoy fresh tasting coffee for hours.
