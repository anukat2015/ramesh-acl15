Title: Best laptop money can buy!
Content:
This HP DV4 is great. Compared to other makes and models it actually the best when considering the price you pay against it's specs. As a matter of fact I actually bought three of the same model. One for me and two for my friends.
In short. If you by this laptop you won't need to buy any external hardware accessories as it has everything you might and might not need.
