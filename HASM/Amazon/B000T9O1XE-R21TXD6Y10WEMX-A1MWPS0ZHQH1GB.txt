Title: Great MP3 Player
Content:
My 5 year old daughter got this for Christmas.  It is easy to download to and easy to use.  We like the fact that there is a built in speaker so that our daughter doesn't have to use the earbuds so we can make sure it's not too loud.  Our daughter loves music and loves to dance, the players design makes it easy to hold while she's dancing around.
