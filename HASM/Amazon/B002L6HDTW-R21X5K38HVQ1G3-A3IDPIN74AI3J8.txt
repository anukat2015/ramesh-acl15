Title: All i Wanted in a music Player!
Content:
Music, Video, radio, and a Video Camera! When i first got this i was mainly thinking about the camera but when i got the radio with it i was wow'ed not to mention i got a really loud speaker with it! Overall, this is easily five stars, not a flaw in the system it self, but more specifically i was disappointed on the camera that you could only take video and above that you can't zoom.
All and all this a must have iProduct.
