Title: Great purchase
Content:
Heats up a full pot quickly and doesn't ever boil.  I actually bought this to make spiced wine in during the winter months.  Does an excellent job and is easy to serve out of with the front nozzle.  Big advantage over using a crock pot.  Cleans up very easy too.
