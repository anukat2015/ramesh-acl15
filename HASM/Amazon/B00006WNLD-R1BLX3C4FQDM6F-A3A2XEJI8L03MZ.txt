Title: B & D Coffee-maker
Content:
This coffee maker makes a very good cup of coffee in rapid time. I find that I must use a funnel to fill it with water. Also, the lid was difficult to separate from the main lid; my husband called your company to be sure we wouldn't break it with too much force.
