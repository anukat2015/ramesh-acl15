Title: Great Little Floor Cleaner  for the Money
Content:
This small, cordless elec. broom is just what we needed to clean up the dog hair on our tile and bamboo floors and also for the indoor/outdoor rug in the sunroom. Also use it on the comforters, dog beds, and sofa/chair cushions. It's so lightweight and effortless, it almost seems like it's not working!  Highly recommend it.
