Title: The best for the price
Content:
There are already so many reviews for this time tested piece that my contribution is not really required. But my say is that If you buy this you will not regret, this is a very powerful piece of equipment and you will enjoy every bit of it.
Although the kit lens is not that great and I wish I had bought another good lens with it, which I am going to do soon.
