Title: Excellent AC
Content:
Quite quiet, cools well, good looking design. The louvers move well,
overall an excellent unit. My only suggestion would be to make the window
fitting parts a bit less flimsy. Nevermind all the complains about 
controls on the unit, and so on -- it has just what it needs. An
overall excellent unit.
