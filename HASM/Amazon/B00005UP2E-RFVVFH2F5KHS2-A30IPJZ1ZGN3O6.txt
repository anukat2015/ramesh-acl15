Title: Just what I needed
Content:
A vacuum is nothing that you purchse often, but when
yours breaks the search is on.  I find this vacuum
perfectly portable for my needs.  I have tile floors,
carpet, rugs, hardwood floors and carpeted steps.  The
wand is long enough, the cord is long enough and the
vacuum is light enough to carry. D. Smith
