Title: Space Heater
Content:
Great space heater - very fast to heat the area around it. On it's high heat setting,  it uses only 800 watts. The oil-filled heater uses 1500 watts and takes a long time to get the space heated - but not this radiant.   Plus one can select the movement to get the heated air all around it faster.
