Title: Makes excellent espressos and cappuccinos!
Content:
We have used this machine at least once a day for 1 year and it never let us down! We always get complements on the espressos and cappuccinos. We seldom use it for regular coffe, though.It does not take much counter space and it's very simple and easy to use and clean.
