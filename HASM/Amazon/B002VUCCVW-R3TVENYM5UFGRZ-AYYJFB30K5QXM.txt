Title: Great idea but no Dell warranty
Content:
Dell Adamo XPS. I have not purchased one yet, still gathering information. I love the concept but wonder why Dell stopped offering them. I spoke with Dell via. chat and they stated "their life had ended, no further information available and Dell will not back a computer bought from a third party". I have yet to find out what Amazon has to say.
