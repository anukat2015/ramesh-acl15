Title: Coby MP3 Player - a nice starter device!
Content:
I have come late to the digital music revolution, and I was able to figure out how to use this MP3 Player with ease.  The sound quality is not quite what I hoped, but for listening to my downloaded tracks while working out or studying, it certainly fits the bill and the price is right. I can easily recommend it.
