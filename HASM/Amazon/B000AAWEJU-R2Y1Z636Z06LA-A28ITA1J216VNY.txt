Title: Miele Canister Vacuum Cleaner
Content:
There are times in your life when you discover that there was something better out there than what you have been used to. The Miele is one of those products that makes you realize that whatever you were using before was an inferior machine. Its lightness and maneuverablity are enough to recommend it  but it also has superior suction and a very quiet motor. This is one fine german import.
