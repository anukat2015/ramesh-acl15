Title: Poor Product; not reliable
Content:
Very disappointed with this product. After using it for a while, only on weekends, the machine stopped working properly. It looks like the temperature sensor went bad; once the drink is hot, the machine  stops, but it will not turn back on to keep the right temperature. I do not recommend.
