Title: Only so-so
Content:
Not big on this vacuum. We have had it for 3 years. I live in a small apartment with 2 kids and a long haired cat. It works ok. The dual clean edge never had any suction. It is very, very loud. I am already in the market for something better. Maybe I'll try a Hoover.
