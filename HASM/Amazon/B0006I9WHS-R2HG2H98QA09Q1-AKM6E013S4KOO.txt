Title: Great heater, but does not last long.
Content:
Heats up a cold bathroom quickly and enough so that when you get out of the shower, it's not freezing.  Broke after 3-4 months.  The thermostat works like it should.  Great heater, but if it lasted 1+ year, it would be a perfect heater.
