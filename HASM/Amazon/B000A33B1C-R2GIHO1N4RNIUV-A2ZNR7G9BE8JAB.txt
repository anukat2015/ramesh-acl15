Title: Good supplemental heater
Content:
A great heater.  We keep it not too far from the thermostat so that [with the built-in timer] we can control the house furnace while keeping the primary living space cozy.  I love the integrated 24-hr timer.  Only wish it had a quick over-ride without having to interfere with the timer.
