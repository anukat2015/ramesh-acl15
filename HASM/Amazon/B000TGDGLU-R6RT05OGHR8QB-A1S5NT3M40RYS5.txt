Title: Long-lasting Heater
Content:
This is the exact heater we borrowed from our parents to heat our baby's room until we wanted to turn the heat higher in the whole house. Theirs was at least 20 years old and in the same great condition as our new heater. It is easy to use, warms his room exactly how much we want to and does not leave any odors or make any sounds like other heaters may do. We love it!
