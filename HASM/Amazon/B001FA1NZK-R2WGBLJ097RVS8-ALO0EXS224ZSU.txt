Title: A fun pda alternative
Content:
Really enjoying this replacement for my old pda. Nice calendar and contact functions.  Had trouble setting up mail but now works great.  Lots of nice free apps.  Apple phone support is limited but I'm close to an Apple Store so they helped me.
Amazon was quick shipping, but their packaging was not adequate for an electronic item!
