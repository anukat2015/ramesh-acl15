Title: Have owned 3 of these for 4 years and love them
Content:
We've owned three of these for four years and love them.  We put them in our bathrooms and use them each cold morning.  We are able to keep the entire house much cooler than we used to because we have these in the bathrooms.  A great value; a nice, thin, attractive design; and very lightweight.
