Title: Good product to listen to music
Content:
4 years ago, I had purchased a nano at the same price that came with 1 gb memory or 256 songs roughly. Now, I purchased this 5th generation nano at the same price, that comes with 8gb memory standard and plus a still/movie camera and better sound reception. 
I like this as I wanted to buy an apple product with no frills and less fancier features. Something that would allow me to listen to music.
