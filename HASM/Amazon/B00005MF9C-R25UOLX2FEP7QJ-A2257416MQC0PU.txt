Title: Great one cup coffee maker.
Content:
I love this coffee maker.  It works great..I have a larger pot that I use in the mornings but this is great for a cup in the afternoon or when I only want a cup in the mornings.  Since I am retired, I haven't used the travel mug yet, instead use my regular cup.
