Title: Love this little heater!
Content:
I absolutely love this little space heater! It is small, powerful, is capable of heating a fairly large room, and is an awesome value for the money. I already purchased two of these heaters, and will be purchasing two more for the future. Definitely give this little heater a try before you spend money on a more expensive one that does not work even half as well.
