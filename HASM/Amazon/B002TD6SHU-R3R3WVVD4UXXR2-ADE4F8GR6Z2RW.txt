Title: Miele S2120 Great Vacuum
Content:
Don't look any further for a better vacuum! This is our second Miele and I have to say this one is better than our first purchased and more expensive Miele! They have made many small improvements, it's more quiet, lighter and has a telescoping handle so you can hide it in a closet. Yet has all the strength and durability of what you would expect from a Miele.
