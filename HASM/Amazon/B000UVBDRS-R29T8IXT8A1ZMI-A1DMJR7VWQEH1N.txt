Title: Better than Ipod???
Content:
Easy to use interface, expandable memory, and great sound.  Has FM tuner, a great screen that plays video, and it also syncs to Outlook.  Customizable equalizer, slideshow option, and voice recorder also help tailor this player to your needs.  About the only thing this little guy doesn't do is play video games.
