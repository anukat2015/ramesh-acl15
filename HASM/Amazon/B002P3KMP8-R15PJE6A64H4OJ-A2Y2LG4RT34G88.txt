Title: Long battery
Content:
Love this laptop! Great screen, 10 hrs. battery life, quick internet access, videos look terrific, audio is a big improvement from my IBM T43--no straining to hear.  It's light and thin.  Overall, couldn't ask for any more. I've used it for a month now and have had no problems. I've already recommended to several friends.
