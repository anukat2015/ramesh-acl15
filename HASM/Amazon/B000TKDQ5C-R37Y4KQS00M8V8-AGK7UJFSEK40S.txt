Title: Heater will run you out of the room!
Content:
I wanted a space heater that would be small for my office but heat up the room.  I also wanted to make sure it is fire safe and kid safe.  It is both.  The only thing I would note is that the front of the unit, where the heat comes out, is hot to the touch.  I definitely recommend this heater.
