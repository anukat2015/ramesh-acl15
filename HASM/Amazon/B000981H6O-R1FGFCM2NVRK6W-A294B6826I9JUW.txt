Title: This thing is awesome. For people with a real mess to clean up !!
Content:
The only reason I gave this 4 out of 5 stars is because you have to hold the button down to use it and it makes your thumb a little tired if you are doing a large area. This handheld is great ! Has lots of power. I have a 2yr old and an 8 month old. This thing has become a lifesaver !!
