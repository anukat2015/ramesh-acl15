Title: Amazing Machine
Content:
I did weeks of research and configured the same specs everywhere (Dell, HP, Asus, Acer, Alienware) and a comparable computer would have been at least $2700 with any other brand.
This thing is FAST and works out of the box unlike the Sony FW i just returned (Whining noise and Bluetooth audio didn't work).
The one minus is that it comes preloaded with a lot of bloatware.
