Title: Tons Of Portable Music
Content:
Extremely thin and beautiful physical design makes this easy to carry with you wherever you go when you want to listen to music, and 8 GBs holds alot of songs.
The screen is just a bit small to watch many videos, but to have the chance to watch videos on a Nano is still a big plu (I have an older Nano and all the new features and size of the new ones made this worthwhile.)
