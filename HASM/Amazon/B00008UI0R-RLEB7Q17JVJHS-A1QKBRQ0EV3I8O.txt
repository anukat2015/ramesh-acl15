Title: Hoover S3765-040 Wind Tunnel Bagless Vacuum
Content:
This is the best canister vacuum I've owned.  It's light and easy to maneuver and the suction is super!  It practically runs itself.  My old vacuum was heavy and cumbersome.  I can honestly say this new vacuum makes all my rugs look like new. It's a great vacuum at a great price!
