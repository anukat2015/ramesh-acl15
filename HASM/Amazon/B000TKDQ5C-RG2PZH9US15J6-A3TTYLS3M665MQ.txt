Title: Hot but noisy
Content:
Pros: It kicks out a lot of heat, is solidly built, has a long cord, and is small in size. Cons: It's a bit on the noisy side (think of it as a "little" roar) and only has one speed for the fan setting. Will it last? We'll see.
