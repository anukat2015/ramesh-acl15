Title: P.O.L. Tank Connector will fit the newwe ACME valves.
Content:
The part number on the package is F273702. It has the P.O.L. style connector for the LP tank. At first glance you will think that it will not work on the newer ACME style LP tank connectors. However, I called Mr. Heater and they showed me how the newer ACME style connectors do still have an internal female thread that will work perfectly with this hose.
