Title: A must have for coffee enthusiasts
Content:
I am very pleased with my Black N Decker Brew and Go coffee maker.  I like the convenience of making just one cup and clean up is really easy.  I also like the fact that you don't need to buy coffee filters.  The coffee remains hot in the cup for much longer than I thought.  Definitely worth having one in the office or in the home. Now go get your drink on !!
