Title: great little room heater
Content:
This is a great deal. It heats quickly and well. I am a bit of a quiet freak and it is very quiet and unobtrusive. the only downside is that the dials are a bit hard to adjust, they are not marked well.  That said, they can be worked with and for the money, this is a great space heater.
