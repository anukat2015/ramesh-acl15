Title: Nice, gets the job done.
Content:
Really like this cute little coffee maker. Definitely gets the job done, and was exactly what I was looking for. Doesn't take up much space. The only thing you have to remember is to turn it off once you're done brewing your cup. Would recommend if you're looking for a coffee maker for yourself, or for one person.
