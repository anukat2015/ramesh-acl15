Title: Disappointed
Content:
The whole purpose of a carafe is to keep the coffee hot. Not with this coffee maker. Unless you drink all 8 cups right after brewing, your coffee will be luke-warm at best. We returned ours after 3 days. I'm assuming that the more expensive models work better.
