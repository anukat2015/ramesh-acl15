Title: Buy this vacuum
Content:
If you're looking for a multi purpose vacuum cleaner your search is over.  The only thing which could possibly do a better job of eliminating dust and allergens in the household is a whole house vacuum which exhausts to the outside.  I've now purchased two Ovygen2 vacuums for two houses.  Stop looking.  Buy this vacuum. (Unless of course you're considering installing a whole house vacuum) The subscription service for bags and filters on the Electrolux site is nice too.
