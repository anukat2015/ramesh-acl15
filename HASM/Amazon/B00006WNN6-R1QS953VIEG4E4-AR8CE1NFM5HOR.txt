Title: Perfect space heater
Content:
I love this little heater! I used it in my office, putting it right on the desk so the heat blows directly on me (I sit under an air vent that makes me freeze). Never had a problem with it. The auto-shutoff feature (when the air temp is warm enough)) works perfectly. I highly recommend this heater.
