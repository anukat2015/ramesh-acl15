Title: IPod Touch is about the same price & I got that instead
Content:
After some research, I decided to buy the IPod Touch instead of the Nano. While the Nano allows for video and audio, the IPod touch has interactivity and capability that far surpasses the Nano. Check the prices and capabilities on both and you may make the same decision.
