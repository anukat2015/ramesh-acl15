Title: 8 years and counting!!!
Content:
I've had mine for at least 8 years and it still works wonderfully! This past summer, it wasn't working too well for awhile. All the water wouldn't get used. I ran a cup of white vinegar through it and it was good as new!!! Terrific value esp considering the price!!!
