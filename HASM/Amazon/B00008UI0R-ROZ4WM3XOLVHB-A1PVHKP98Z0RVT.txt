Title: Hoover Windtunnel Vacuum
Content:
The body of the vacuum is very light in weight which I was looking for but didnt realize that there would be difficulty in manuevering it across the floor or rugs...The carpet/floor appliance should be two separate pieces as the height and width of it cause problems getting in corners and under furniture.  Overall I like the vacuum's suction but might consider something different next time.
