Title: excellent!
Content:
I've had this product for over 10 years, but in the course of moving, it was damaged. That is the only reason I purchased a new one. It is the best kitchen appliance I've ever had. I highly recommend this product to everyone interested in having a hot, and fast drink, whether it be instant coffee, soup, cocoa, etc. It is convenient, safe and easy to operate, and you have a hot drink in a little over a minute. I highly recommened this product to anyone who wishes to have a nice hot beverage at the touch of a button.
