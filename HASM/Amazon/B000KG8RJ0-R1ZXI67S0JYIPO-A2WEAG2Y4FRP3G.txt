Title: GREAT Heater
Content:
This heater is even better than i thought. The supply line is thick and pliable, the 3 temp settings work great and at the 55 setting it has no problem heating my uninsulated 20x20 garage in 30 degree weather. This is my second Desa heater and this one blows my old 35,000 btu one away.
