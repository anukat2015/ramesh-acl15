Title: Great laptop for great price
Content:
I had an HP and Dell laptop,wanted to try a different one.
Was searching for a laptop mainly for surfing.
Was lucky to get this one for $542+shipping in Lightning Deals.
Pros:
-Core2Duo 2.2Ghz
-4 GB RAM
-500 GB HD
-Built in Webcam with mic
-Lightweight & less noise
-Windows 7
Cons:
-No seprate keys for mute,play ,stop e.t.c.
-R.Click and L.click is not smooth
-Speakers at the front bottom.
Overall this is a great laptop from ASUS and definitely will 
recommend others to buy this.
