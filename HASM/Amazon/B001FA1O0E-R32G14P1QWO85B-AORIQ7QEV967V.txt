Title: Great hardware but even better software via App Store
Content:
We love this device!  It works very well.  Handles most anything.  Check out the iTunes app store for thousands of applications that greatly enhance your iPod touch experience.  I am sure you will find something you will like.  Games, business applications, social networking applications for your FaceBook.  Endless choices.  You won't regret this purchase.
