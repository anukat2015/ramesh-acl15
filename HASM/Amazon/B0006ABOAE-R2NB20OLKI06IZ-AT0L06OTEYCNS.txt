Title: Gross Coffee
Content:
We were so excited about this machine. Now, three months later I wish it was never made.
The espresso is just so-so. Everything that comes out of this machine tastes like plastic. Which means that plastic is going into your system. And thats not good, my friend, not good.
The coffee it makes is horrible.
