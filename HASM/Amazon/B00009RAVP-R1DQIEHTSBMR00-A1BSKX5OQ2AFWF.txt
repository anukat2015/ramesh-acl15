Title: Best heater I have ever bought
Content:
Great heater!  I have tried several radiant heaters and fan heaters.  This one beats them all.  The orange glow is very pleasant, it works silently, and the controls are clear and well marked.  The lowest heat setting is good enough for me.  Worth every penny.
