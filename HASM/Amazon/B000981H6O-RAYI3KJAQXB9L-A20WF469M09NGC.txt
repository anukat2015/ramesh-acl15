Title: My Wife Is Happy, So I Am Happy
Content:
My wife had an old model Hoover Hand Vac for about 8 years.  It finally died and I tried to replace it with the old model.  I couldn't find it so I bought a Black&Decker.  My wife hated it!  Too big and too noisy.  I ordered the Hoover S1120.  It didn't have a crevice tool or other attachments, but the S1120 was close enough to the old one to make her happy.
