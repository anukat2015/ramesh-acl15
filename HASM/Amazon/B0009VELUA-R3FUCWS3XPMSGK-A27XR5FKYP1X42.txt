Title: Great coffee maker!
Content:
This coffee maker is easy to program and use! Considering the amount of money paid for the coffee maker, I would have liked for the water resevoir to be more user friendly. Had this for many years...If it was not a gift, I am not sure I would have bought it for so much money though.
