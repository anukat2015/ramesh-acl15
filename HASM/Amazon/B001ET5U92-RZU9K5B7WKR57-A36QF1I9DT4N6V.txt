Title: The thing just takes great pictures
Content:
I've had my D90 for a couple of weeks now, and I can't say that I understand most of the features and whiz-bang stuff the camera can do, but it sure does take nice pictures.  For example, I was at the 2009 Westminster Dog Show last night, and I took some shots with the D90 and the 18-200 Nikon lens using only the light in Madison Square Garden (no flash).  I'm sure the pros sitting on the Garden floor got better results, but I was really happy with my results.  Take a look if you want to see how this camera performs in "real life" in the hands of a hobbiest.
