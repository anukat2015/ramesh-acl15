Title: Poor thermostat control
Content:
Heater and fan work fine.  Adjustable thermostat is poor; wide dead band with temperature getting very warm before it turns off, then very cold before it turns on again.  No safety cutout, so if heater accidently gets knocked over; the heater stays ON.
