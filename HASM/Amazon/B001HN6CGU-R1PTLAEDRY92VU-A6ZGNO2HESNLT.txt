Title: not for audiobook lovers
Content:
I purchased this mp3 player for my 9 year old granddaughter.  She enjoys music downloads. However, she was very disappointed to find that it does not recognize library audiobook format.  This was one of the main features she wanted to use.  It seems that some Coby players are formated for audiobooks but not the mp610.
