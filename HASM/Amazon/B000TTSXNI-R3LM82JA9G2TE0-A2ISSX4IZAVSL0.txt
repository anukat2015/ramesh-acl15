Title: Helped to cut our natural Gas bill
Content:
Great product, using this heater has helped to cut our natural gas bill by 30%! Heats well but it is a bit annoying that it starts out on high so if you want to lower it you have to cycle through all the settings first since it doesn't go to lowest but mid setting after hitting temp control.
I would recommend this product highly!
