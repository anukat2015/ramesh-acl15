Title: Hoover Wind Tunnel Canister Vacuum Cleaner- Hepa
Content:
This is a great little canister vacuum cleaner with variable suction control, it has all of the necessary attachments such as a crvice tool, floor cleaner, and upholstery brush.  It is lightweight, easy to carry, and does not require a bag.  Cleaning seems to be quicker with this vacuum than prior one.  I really recommend it to anyone and it also has a very neat futuristic styling to it.
