Title: Excellent for hardwood floors
Content:
This is gentle on my wood floors.
It comes with a power head for carpets but I don't speak to that. The hardwood floor attachment is highly maneuverable. A twist of the wrist and it fits almost everywhere negating the need to move stuff. It goes all the skinny way under the bed. ALL the way!
Great vacuum.
