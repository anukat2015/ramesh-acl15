Title: Great Camera-Not for beginners
Content:
Such an awesome camera, but beginners beware.  It has more options than one can figure out.  I have read the instructions about 4 times, and still am trying to figure out things.  Takes an amazing picture, which is all that truly matters.  Recommend for cameraman who knows what there doing.
