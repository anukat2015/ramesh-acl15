Title: Vornado Vortex Heater
Content:
I bought this to replace the old one that finally gave up the ghost.  I like the digital but found 1 thing which was a problem.  When you have a power failure, you have to turn it back on again. The old one was on with a mechanical switch and when the power came back on, so did the heater.  I can't rely on this one for those times when I need the heat after the power is restored and I am not there at the time to turn it back on.
