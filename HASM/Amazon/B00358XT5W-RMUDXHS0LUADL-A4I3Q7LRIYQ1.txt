Title: Best bang for buck.
Content:
I purchased this laptop for my cousin. I was not looking for a high performance laptop since my cousin wouldn't be doing any intense computing on any computer..:) This laptop has awesome specs for its price with a beautiful HD display. Aesthetically, this laptop is sleek and attractive. I would definitely recommend this laptop to persons on a budget that's looking for a laptop with adequate performance.
