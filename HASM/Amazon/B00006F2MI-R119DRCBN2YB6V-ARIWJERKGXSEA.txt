Title: Great Gift
Content:
I purchased this idem for my sister for her birthday as she was complaining her old coffee maker wasn't keeping her coffee hot enough.  She loves this coffee maker, not only does the thermal coffee pot keep it piping hot but she enjoys the timer that starts the coffee brewing before she's out of bed and ready to pour when she walks into the kitchen.
