Title: Zen
Content:
The Zen Micro Photo 8 GB MP3 Player is terrific.  It is easy to use both for adding songs and play back.  The menu navigation is well done.
A few days after our MP3 player arrived it died.  There was no misuse.  Amazon sent us a replacement with no difficulty.  I suppose that was a fluke.
