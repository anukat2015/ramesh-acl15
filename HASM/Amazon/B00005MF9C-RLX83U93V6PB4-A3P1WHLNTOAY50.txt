Title: A perfect one-cup
Content:
This little coffee machine is great - I highly recommend it for folks like me who just want one cup to walk out the door with!  I've found that many of my travel mugs and ceramic mugs alike fit the machine, so I'm not bound to using only the mug provided with the coffeemaker.  The coffee is always plenty hot, and the auto shut-off is great.   A+!
