Title: BUNN COFFEE POT
Content:
Bunn NHB Professional Home Brewer, Black  The only reason I purchased this pot was because the coffee was to be extra hot.  I am terribly disappointed.  It comes out very quickly- great! but once the cream is added, it is almost lukewarm unless I warm the cream first.  Even then, the coffee is not as hot as the one that comes from McDonalds (ex.)
