Title: As light as a netbook but better...
Content:
Great little mini-notebook that's better than my netbook.  I wish this product had been available back when I bought my netbook.  With the Acer Aspire AS1410 I don't need a netbook.  Faster processor, more memory, larger hard drive and 11.6" monitor.  The new models with a dual-core processor should be even better.
