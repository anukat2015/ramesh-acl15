Title: Amazing Machine!
Content:
This machine does it all.  It's sleek design makes it even more enjoyable to use than the iPhone which is slightly larger in thickness.  This is one great device.  Nothing else on the market even comes close to this.  Be sure to buy at least a 32gig if you plan on putting movies on it as movies take up lots of memory.  I highly reccomend this to anyone.  I bought it for my six year old and she works it like a pro.  It was her favorite Christmas present!
