Title: LOVE this machine
Content:
Love this machine!  It brews the perfect cup of coffee each time and in almost no time at all.  There are so many choices of coffee (brands and flavors) that everyone is satisfied.  It does work better with filtered water, and although the cup size is limited to basically a small and medium, that is the only negative for me.  I highly recommend this machine!
