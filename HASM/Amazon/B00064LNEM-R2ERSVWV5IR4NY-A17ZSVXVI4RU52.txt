Title: Excellent basic coffee maker
Content:
After reading most of the reviews of this product on Amazon, I bought this product as a replacement for a mr coffee that had sprung a leak. This is an excellent basic coffee maker. It brews quickly, Makes good coffee, and keeps it very hot. I am very pleased with this product.
