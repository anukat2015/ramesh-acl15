Title: Low profile heater, safe heat.
Content:
Size is right and only the front gets warm. Great for closed spaces and you don't worry about catching anything on fire. Only downside is it doesn't put out quite as much heat as you would like. Takes away the chill, but does not keep you as toasty as you might like.
