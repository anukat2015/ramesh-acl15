Title: wonderful heater
Content:
I moved into a large older home and was concerned about comfort and gas bills. I set it up between the living room and dining room and let it rotate to distribute the heat.  Works like a charm and I am able to keep the thermostat lower.  Haven't received my first bills yet but expect the heater to help level out the utilities.
