Title: My experience and suggestion.
Content:
Have a great results with old model "HMHR-1101 which I still using. So I decide to set up another one in my apt., and thought the new model(it's come with fan) should be better. Unfortunately this model does not have wick pre-installed inside (Product Descriptions' hasn't mention it as well). I would suggest whoever plan to buy this should get a package of replacement wick.
