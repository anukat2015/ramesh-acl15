Title: Vacuum cleaner
Content:
This product is exactly what I was looking for.  It works just as well on carpets as it does on hardwood floors.  The only drawback is vacuuming the stairs.  You need to have one hand on the vacuum cleaner so it doesn't fall.  But the suction is great and it gets the job done.
