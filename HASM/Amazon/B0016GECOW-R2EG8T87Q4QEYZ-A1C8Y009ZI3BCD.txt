Title: Warning
Content:
This product looks great, but seems to be just a piece of non functioning plastic.  The idea of it is pretty great, light and simple.  However, the one I received did not work.  I tried it on 4 computers, all did not recognize it and thus could not use it.  I called Coby and they just told me that its a great product and that I just happened to get a defective one and that they could offer me no help.  Luckily, Amazon let me return it for a refund.  So if you are willing to take a chance that it might work for you, then go for it, but if not I would not choose this product.
