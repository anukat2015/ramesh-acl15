Title: One great coffee maker
Content:
I belong to a coffee club at work and we burned the old coffee maker out.  Not only does everyone love the coffee maker's beautiful red color they love the performance. The pulse serve actually works without the slightest of drip unto the hot plate and the carafe performs equally as well with no drips.  It makes coffee very quickly and effortlessly. This has turned out to be one of the best internet purchases I've ever made.
