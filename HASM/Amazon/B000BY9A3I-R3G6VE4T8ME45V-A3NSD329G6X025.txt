Title: great vacuum
Content:
The dirt devil 082700 vacuum is a great vacuum. Just what I was looking for. It is light weight, great suction and bagless. I have mostly hard wood floors and tile floors. I dont think it would be great on thick  carpets, but for short carpets it works very well. I am very please with this vacuum.
