Title: No battery life
Content:
The Nomad advertises 16 hours battery life. IT IS ONLY 3 HOURS!! I talked to one of their techs and he admitted it's only about 3 hours on a full charge. This is so bad because you can't even pop in AA's. WOULD NOT BUY THIS BECAUSE OF THE POOR BATTERY LIFE!
