Title: Seems to work well
Content:
I have been using this to keep a non heated work room from being TOO cold. The unit does cycle on and off alot so I am not sure if it is that energy efficient. However, I just put the unit on one of the lowest setting since the room is not used that much, and the unit does keep the room from being ICE cold. So it does work for what I need to do and I have had no troubles from it thus far.
