Title: Soccer Dad's answer for Heat
Content:
This heater is the perfect answer to portable heat while attending daughter's soccer games!  So easy to take along with the soccer blanket & chair & no worries for any hookups.  Through all the wind & rain & cheering on the field line the heater provides warmth but only if you sit in your chair long enough with it beside you.
