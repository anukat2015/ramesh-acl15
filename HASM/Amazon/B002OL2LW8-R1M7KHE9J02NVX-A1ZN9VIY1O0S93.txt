Title: Nice computer, but....
Content:
This is a really good computer, met all my expectations except there is no HDMI port.  Its there and covered with plastic.  After a month of going back and forth with the seller and Toshiba.  I got confirmation that this model M505-4790 DOES NOT COME WITH HDMI.  so its not a fluke that many other reviews have said that.  Im disappointed that Amazon hasn't actually fixed this in the description to be accurate.   Overall I'm still keeping the comptuer cause it was an excellent deal and with the VGA I can still get awesome pic and sound on the TV.
