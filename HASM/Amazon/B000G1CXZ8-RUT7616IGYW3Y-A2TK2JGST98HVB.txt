Title: Disappointing Heater
Content:
This heater provides a good amount of heat.  However, it is noisy on the high setting, the thermostat works poorly and anytime you adjust the heater, it emits an extremely loud and annoying beeping sound that cannot be turned off or down.  I would look elsewhere...
