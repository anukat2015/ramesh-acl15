Title: Amazing Little Heater!
Content:
This heater really IS amazing!  It's used to heat a pretty good sized common area. And you really KNOW this bugga is not only heating the room, but the hallways, galley kitchen area and all this in a 3 bdrm/2 bath place!  
You really KNOW it's working when you open any closed door to enter adjoining room and you see how COLD it is in there!  When i first experienced this I was shocked to see how it was actually heating the entire area. And we don't run this high (around 72 max) for this to be the case. It makes life tolerable in the cold season. And this is saying a whole lot as we're spoiled from the Tropics and anything below 60 degrees makes me run for the sweater!  
Amazed? You bet.
