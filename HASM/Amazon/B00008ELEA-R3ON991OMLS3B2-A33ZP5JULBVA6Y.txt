Title: Convenient, Fast, and Easy-to-Clean Coffee Maker
Content:
The Farberware FCP240 Electric Perolator (2-4 cup model) meets the needs I have in a coffee maker for a single cup of coffee in the morning. It is easy to set up, makes a decent cup of coffee, and is easy to clean. Moreover, it takes little space on my kitchen counter.
