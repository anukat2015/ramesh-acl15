Title: Love My Fuze!!
Content:
I love my Fuze!  It was SO easy to set up!  I don't have the time or patience to sit and try to figure out gadgets.  I didn't have a problem with this at all.  I absolutely loved not paying for an IPod.  The price was right, it's the perfect size, it's easy to use AND I can add memory (and already have).  What more could you want?  I definitely recommend this little gem!
