Title: Great little heater
Content:
A great little heater that warms up big spaces. We use it to take the chill out of a large area of the house or to really heat up a room. An early am swim in an unheated pool can chill you to the bone, but put this heater in the bathroom before you hit the water and a sauna awaits you at the finish line. Nice.
