Title: Love the Cuisinart DGB-650BC
Content:
Best coffee I've ever tasted.  Love the automatic grind feature.  Although, it would be nice if it had an alarm or system to tell you that you haven't cleaned grinds from past usage.  I've spilled some grind coffee because I forgot to check filter basket.
Design is a cleaner and better than the previous 600 unit.
AA+++++
