Title: Great All Around Laptop For Any User
Content:
I've had this laptop for several months now and I can honestly say that I am happy with it. The processor is quick - usually running several programs without any slowing at all. Movie playback is great - either watching from a dvd or streaming from the net. Nice bright and glossy screen, reminds me of a smaller plasma tv screen. Overall, a great laptop for any user.
