Title: a music discovery engine
Content:
The device is beautiful, the interface is slick, the software is useful and a joy to use. Sure, it doesn't have the sort of app support that the iTouch does, but I think that the Zune was not made to occupy the nebulous sort-of-a-pda/not-really-a-smartphone place Apple's device does. 
The Zune HD is a MP3 player through and through.
