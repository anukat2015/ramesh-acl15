Title: grind and brew coffee maker
Content:
The coffee maker is a handsome addition to our kitchen counter top. Using fresh ground beans instead of already ground coffee is not marketing hype. The coffee does have a richer flavor. A few more parts to clean because of the grinder but it is not a hassle.
