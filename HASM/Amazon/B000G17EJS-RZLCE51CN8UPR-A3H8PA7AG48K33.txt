Title: Solid Heater
Content:
I needed to get a new space heater for a cold bathroom when the switch on the old one broke.  Although the DUH1100T's utilitarian appearance looks more like something you would use in a basement or garage, it performs very well anywhere.  It has a 4 hour timer, 6 levels of heating and 2 power ranges.  This space heater is easy to use and warms up the room very quickly.  I have been very pleased with it, and it works better than the space heater it replaced.
