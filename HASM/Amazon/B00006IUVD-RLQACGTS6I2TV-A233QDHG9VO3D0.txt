Title: Single cup coffee maker
Content:
Black & Decker Brew "N Go Coffeemaker  -  I purchased mine many years ago so I decided to get another to have at my sister's place in California. It hasn't changed much over the years  -  still easy to operate and very handy to have. I have not used my regular coffee maker or my Tassimo in a long time.
I really believe you would find it very useful to have this single serve coffee maker.
