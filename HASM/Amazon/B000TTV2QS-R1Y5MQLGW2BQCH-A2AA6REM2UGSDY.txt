Title: Great heater!
Content:
This space heater works great!  It oscillates if you wish, which really helps to spread the heat around a room.  Also like the two different energy settings as well as a timer function to have the heater automatically shut off after a specified period of time.  All things considered, for the money I don't think you'll find a better heater to help heat up your room!
