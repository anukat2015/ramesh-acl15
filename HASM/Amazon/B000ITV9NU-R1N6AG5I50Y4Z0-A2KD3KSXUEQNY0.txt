Title: AntOnline Not Cooperative
Content:
The heater arrived with the box having been opened & resealed.  In the description, there was no mention of the extra 5 inches of space required for safety all around the unit.  I contacted AntOnline regarding swapping and they referred me to World Distributors, who referred me back to AntOnline.  Bottom line, neither would exchange, leaving me with a heater I can't use.
