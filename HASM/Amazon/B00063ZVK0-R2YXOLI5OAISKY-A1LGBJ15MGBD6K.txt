Title: Good Things in Small Packages
Content:
Am thrilled with the efficiency of this product. Bought it to heat one room only, farthest away from HVAC system with a concrete slab room bordering garage, etc. It works extremely well and the heat even gets pushed into other rooms. No need to run it continuously when we're in the room. Caveat: temps have not gotten below 30 yet! It's not winter, but so far, I'm pleased with this little guy.
