Title: Good Value
Content:
I own a 32GB 3G iPod Touch and love it. But will not risk taking it to the gym or some other places. So I started looking for an additional low cost mp3 player that will not cost much but offer a lot.
This iPod does just that. Excellent audio, good capacity, many many extras like pedometer video playback and ease of use. 
I am happy with this little guy.
