Title: Don't buy this to vacuum carpets
Content:
I bought this vacuum to vacuum my carpets.  I wish I hadn't. I wish I had read these reviews before I bought it. It is the worst vacuum I have ever had.  It does not pick up at all.  A complete waste of money.  Color is pretty but that's about the best thing about it!
