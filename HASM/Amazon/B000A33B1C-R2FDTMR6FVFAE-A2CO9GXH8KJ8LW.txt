Title: Possible Fire Hazard
Content:
I purchased this heater to supplement the heat in my home.  After 3 months, it started to turn the cord and outlet really warm.  It finally tripped the breaker, and when the breaker was reset, it started to catch fire.  Needless to say, it went to the dump...
