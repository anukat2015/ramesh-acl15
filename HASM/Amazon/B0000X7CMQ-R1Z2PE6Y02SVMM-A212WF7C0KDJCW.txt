Title: Great!
Content:
The Zojirushi Fresh Brew Coffee Maker is great! Its quick, easy and makes great tasting piping hot coffee!  I was nervous at first about bying an unknown brand but decided to try it anyway.  It came within a week after ordering it and I felt it was very easy to use.  I can make coffee in the morning at 7 and still have hot coffee at 5 pm when I'm getting ready to go back to work!
