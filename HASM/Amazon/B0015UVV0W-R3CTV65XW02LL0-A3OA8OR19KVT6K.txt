Title: Disappointing Performance
Content:
This heater has one good quality--it is quiet.  Unfortunately, the thing it does not do well is heat.  At the HI setting there was just the barest zephyr of warm air coming out, perhaps the unit was defective.  There seems to be just one loop of wire heating element that just does not seem adequate to heat any size room. Units costing a small fraction of the Vornado perform as well or better.
