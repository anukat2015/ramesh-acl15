Title: Great Product
Content:
Bought this product to replace a model we bought from another company.  This one completely fill the need!  We like to turn down the heat in the house at night, why heat the place for no reason, and keep this in our room to keep the room heated nicely.  It works perfectly.  The heat just pours from the thing and it is completely silent!  No fans or fear of setting anything on fire.  The 5 degree nights are nothing to laugh at here and this has gone a long way!
