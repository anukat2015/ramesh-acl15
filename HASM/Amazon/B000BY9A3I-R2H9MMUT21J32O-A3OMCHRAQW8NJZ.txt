Title: great little vacuum
Content:
I have tile and hardwood floors throughout my house.  Add 3 cats and frequent vacuuming is necessary.  This Dirt Devil is easy to get out and does an awesome job.  Additionally, it works well on the area rugs I have, along with upholstry.  It's a great vacuum - think I'll give some out as Christmas presents.
