Title: Does it all for an Ultra Portable
Content:
Great computer but don't be fooled by the description. This laptop does NOT come with the 12 hour battery. It comes with the 10 hour 4400mAh battery and Amazon refuses to change the title. I do get about 6.5 hours of run time under normal use.
