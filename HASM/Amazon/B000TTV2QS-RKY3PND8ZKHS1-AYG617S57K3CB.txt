Title: Perfect for my home Office
Content:
I have used small ceramic heaters in my offices at work and at home. When the one at home died, I bought this Lasko 755320 Tower Heater and I have to say, it's fabulous! It heats the room faster and more completely than the little heaters and I love, love, love the remote control.
