Title: Best Christmas present ever!
Content:
I received this for Christmas and it's the best present for someone who lives in a cold climate! I use it everyday!!! It makes the best, creamiest hot cocoa ever. Cappuccinos and lattes rival that of the boutique coffee places and the owner's manual lists about 30 different recipes. You can try something new everyday. 
A fabulous gift or a smart buy for yourself.
