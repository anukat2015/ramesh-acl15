Title: Great Coffee Machine
Content:
I purchased this for work, as I have the same unit for home. The choices in coffees and teas for use in the machne have greatly increased. It is simple to operate, and there is no miss to worry about. And best of all I don't have to risk the chance of having a cup of paint removing liquid that someone is trying to pass of as coffee from the community pot.
