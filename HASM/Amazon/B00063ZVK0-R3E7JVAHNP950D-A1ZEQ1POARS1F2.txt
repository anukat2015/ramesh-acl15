Title: Great heater!!
Content:
I have had it for 2 1/2 months and use it a couple times a week and it is wonderful!  Before we used to huddle around a heater, but now there are no cold spots, or corners in the room, so no matter where we are, we feel warm!  I transport it to any room we may be in and we are comfortable.  We no longer use our house heater! If you can afford it, it is worth the money.  I would definitely purchase it again.
