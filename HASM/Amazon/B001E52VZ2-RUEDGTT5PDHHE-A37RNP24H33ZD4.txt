Title: Excellent value and performance
Content:
I just received this vacuum and I'm already in love. The suction power is so incredible and above it all it has a long hose so you can cover a large area of cleaning without having to pull on the canister every few steps. I tried it on the carpet and floor and it does an excellent job on both.
Amazing power for such a compact appliance. I would recommend it highly.
