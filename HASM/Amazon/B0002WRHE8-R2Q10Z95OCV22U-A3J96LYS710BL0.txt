Title: Fantastic Heater
Content:
My husband bought this heater as an alternate heat source for his hunting cabin.  He said it did a fantastic job.  It was cold and windy outside but he stayed warm and comfortable.  It only took a short time for it to heat the cabin up.  He said that it was well worth the money.
