Title: Great Purchase!
Content:
I ordered this Nano for my granddaughter and she loves it. She has it everywhere she goes and her mom and dad got her a "dock" for her bedside table. It was a bit harder for her grandpa to operate at first, but he learned. Appears to be operating as advertised and is reliable so far.  All in all - a good purchase.
