Title: Great Coffeemaker!
Content:
I love this coffeemaker. It takes about ten minutes to perk 10 very hot, flavorful, and smooth cups of coffee. The taste is the same day after day and beats any drip coffee maker that I have ever used. Excellent price well worth the money.
