Title: Better hope you never need service
Content:
This is a very nice computer at a very good price.  However, if you buy it, you better hope you never need service.  When you call, good  luck!  I will never buy another Acer product because of the complete lack  of  service.  Tech support is utterly useless, and the web site really isn't much help.
