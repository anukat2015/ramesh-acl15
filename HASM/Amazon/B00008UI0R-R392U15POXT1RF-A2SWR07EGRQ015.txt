Title: Good carpet vacuum
Content:
Moving from Europe to US it was hard to find a good vacuum matching the excellent performance of our Bosch vacuum. This vacuum is ideal for thick US-type carpets, but on the hardwood floors it does not convince me to be a good vacuum cleaner. So that's why 3 stars only.
