Title: Great heater
Content:
This heater works great, but only for a single room at a time. I live in a two bedroom apartment and only one of those two rooms is used at night. So what I do is turn down the thermostat to about 60-62 and use the heater in the room with the door closed. It keeps it nice and warm.
