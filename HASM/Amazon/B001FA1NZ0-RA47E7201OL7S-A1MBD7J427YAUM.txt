Title: Great product as long as you don't sweat...
Content:
This is a great little MP3 player, but don't think about using it for running or working out if you plan to sweat.  The headphones are faulty and the unit goes bizerk if you sweat on them.  Search the reviews.  Hundreds of people having the same issue...
