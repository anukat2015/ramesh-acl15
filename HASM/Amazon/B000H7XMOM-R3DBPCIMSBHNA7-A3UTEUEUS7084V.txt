Title: Nice COLD air
Content:
It gets the job done, but its pretty big.
The two fan speeds put out super noise, so I'm going to have to return it, I'm just trying to cool an avg size room, so this one just won't work for me. 
It does do a great job at putting out cold air, so if you got a big room and can take the noise, you won't be sorry....
