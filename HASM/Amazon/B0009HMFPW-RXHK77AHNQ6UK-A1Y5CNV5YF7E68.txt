Title: Simplicity
Content:
If you purchase the Cozy Legs you will find that the supplied stand is very akward.  The stand included with the Cozy Legs requires tools and has to be adjusted just right for it to work well.  This stand is exceedingly simple and should be what is included with the heater.  Save yourself and others a lot of frustration and buy the stand.
