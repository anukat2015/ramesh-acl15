Title: ALMOST a great product
Content:
I've had this machine for about 18 months and while looking for a new machine, figured I should review this one.
This machine has everything I want, makes great coffee and espresso - but it leaks.  I read through reviews here and elsewhere, and it seems to be a design flaw in this and other similar models.  I have to wipe up the puddle of water with every pot of drip coffee that I make.
Now I'm trying to find a machine that does what this one does, but doesn't leak!
