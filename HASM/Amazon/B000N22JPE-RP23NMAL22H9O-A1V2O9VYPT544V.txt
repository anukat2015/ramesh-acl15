Title: Cuisinart Coffeemaker Review
Content:
I am extremely pleased with the red Cuisinart coffeemaker, so much so that I subsequently ordered the red Cuisinart toaster, expecting it would match.  To my extreme disappointment on 10/15/08, the bright orange-red toaster arrived and does not match the dark red coffeemaker.  The toaster is in the process of being returned.  Why not make the appliances matching?
