Title: This machine changed my life
Content:
My coffee is very important to me.
I was surprised to have received this as a gift because I really liked my French Press.
After reading the reviews, I decided to try it and I love it.  I have all the different flavors and types of K cups so the whole family can drink what they want.   
It is more expensive for coffee but I never get coffee out anymore.  This is better!
