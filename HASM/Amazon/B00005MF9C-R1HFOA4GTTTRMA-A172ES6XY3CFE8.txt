Title: Funny taste
Content:
This coffeemaker is very quick brewing - that is nice.  However, I find I cannot drink coffee directly from mug, as it has a rubber like taste.  I pour it into a regular ceramic mug, and the taste is much improved.  For speed it cannot be beat.
