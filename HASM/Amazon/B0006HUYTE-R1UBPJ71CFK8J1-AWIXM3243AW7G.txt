Title: Cannister vac
Content:
I am really enjoying my commercial quality Electrolux.  This is a great lightweight highly efficient vac.  I had a similiar vac before but this one has a more powerful suction, a very flexible but non kinking hose.  You can actually empty the disposable bag at least one time for reuse.  Great product and I highly recommend this.  ron
