Title: Piece of Junk
Content:
This coffee maker is poorly designed and the carafe drips, no pours down the front of the carafe when attempting to pour a cup of coffee.  The base is top heavy and when filled with water is prone to tipping over.  Be prepared to sop up coffee off your table top whenever you pour a cup.
