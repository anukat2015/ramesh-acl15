Title: Makes a Great Gift for the Hard to Please Person on Your List
Content:
I live for coffee... it runs through my veins and keeps my heart pumping so I can tip-tap away on the keyboard... As a culinary historian I am also the foodie author of CALIFORNIA PIONEERS, THEIR STORIES, CULTURE AND CUISINE... and while this nifty gadget by B&D cannot take the place of real campfire coffee perked up over an outdoor fire...However...this is one heck of a great little coffeemaker. I gave one as a gift to a bachelor friend and it was perfect for his lifestyle...He rolls out of bed in the morning for work with not a minute to spare. One flip of the switch and his travel mug of coffee is ready to rush out the door with him.
