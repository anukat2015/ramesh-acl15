Title: Great item
Content:
I bought several of these for the office staff, everyone was delighted as it provides just enough heat to take away the drafty winter feeling.   When opening one package I found they had omitted the velcro mounting adhesive.  I attempted to contact them by email to request that it be mailed to me, using the email address provided in the directions, but received the reply that my email had been unopened and deleted. Other than that, this is a great product.
