Title: This "heater" doesn't!
Content:
This heater doesn't heat. I had an older model Vornado that was great.  Unfortunately, in the newer models the thermostat turns off at the highest setting even though the room is still quite cold.  When the thermostat goes off the fan just circulates cold air.  Freezing here...
