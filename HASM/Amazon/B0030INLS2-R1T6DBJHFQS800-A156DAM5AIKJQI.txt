Title: Toshiba Laptop review
Content:
My second Toshiba laptop. The last one worked for almost 8 years before the hard drive failed. 
This new laptop feels sturdy, is lightning fast compared to the old one, as you can imagine (4 GB vs. 512 MB RAM), definitely a lot of computer for the price. Better than promised, the laptop was delivered in a very short time with no problems.
