Title: I love this laptop
Content:
This is surely a great laptop for a reasonable price when compared to other brands with the same configuration. The key board is smooth. The web cam and the screen display is very good. It has got everything. The only thing, it is little heavier but this can be overlooked.
All together this a worth laptop for the money you pay.
