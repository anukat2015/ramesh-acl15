Title: Keurig coffee brewing system
Content:
Since we received this unit we have been using it multiple times a day.  Its wonderful to always be able to get a fresh brewed cup of coffee.  We have experimented with different manufacturers of Kcup coffee and the quality of the kcup blends is huge.  But, once we found our favorites, we keep going back to them.  The only down fall is the price per cup of the coffee is much more than if we were brewing pots of coffee, but the quality and freshness is so far outwaying the additional cost.
