Title: Perfection!
Content:
The whole purchasing and shipping process went without a hitch. I just LOVE this coffeemaker.  It makes a fantastic cup of coffee and looks classy sitting on my kitchen counter.  It's so easy to use and program and I love the automatic turn off after 4 hours. Also very easy to keep clean and maintain.  Very little can make me smile at 4:45am when I stumble into the kitchen, but this little wonder does it!
