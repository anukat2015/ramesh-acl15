Title: Holmes Ceramic heater
Content:
This little space heater gave off an impressive amount of heat, which could be adjusted by the "High," "Low" and numerical temperature settings.  It was also very quiet to run in the bedroom.  This heater saved me the expense of heating my entire house by just heating the room I was in.  I definitely recommend it.
