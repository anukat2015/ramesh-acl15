Title: Farberware Perculator
Content:
What a wonderful product.  I bought this for my son-in-law as a last try before he quit drinking coffee. He could not find anyway to make a 'good' cup of coffee and the brand didn't seem to matter. This electric perk made the difference between a 'good' cup versus the 'perfect' cup.
