Title: Sexy little power monster
Content:
I love this vacuum.  The only thing to get used to is the small 'mouth' opening, which makes it a little more difficult to clean up large messes.  But it's just as powerful as my old DustBuster and I prefer the ergonomics of this one.  Doesn't hold as much dirt, but that's the price you pay for beauty, I suppose.
