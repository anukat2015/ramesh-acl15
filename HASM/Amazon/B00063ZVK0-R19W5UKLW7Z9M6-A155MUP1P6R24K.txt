Title: Vortex heats 15x16 room
Content:
I have to keep this heater on the LO setting because of old wiring in a very old house.  This heater gives me that option and I keep it set at 64 (in my kids' room).  The heater heats evenly and saves its own electricity by only having the heat on at the level that it is needed.
