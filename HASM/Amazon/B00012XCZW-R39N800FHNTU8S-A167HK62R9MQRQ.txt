Title: Weak Coffee
Content:
If you like strong coffee dont buy this machine. Yes it makes smooth coffee but its weak.  I even bought Extra Bold coffee, whole bean,and ground it myself, from Starbucks and used more than 2 times the normal ammount! The coffee was still on the weak side. VERY dissapointing!
