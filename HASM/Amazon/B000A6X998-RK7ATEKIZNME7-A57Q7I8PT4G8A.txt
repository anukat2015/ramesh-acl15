Title: Great machine
Content:
Does the job of warming a small space perfectly.  I use it by my TV chair in the evenings, or aimed at the kitchen counter area (where I drink my coffee) in the morning--takes the chill off nicely.  Shuts off if you tip it even a bit--so very safe, and doesn't take up a lot of floor space.
