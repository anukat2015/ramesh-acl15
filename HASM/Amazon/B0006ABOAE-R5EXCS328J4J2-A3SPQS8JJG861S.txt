Title: coffee maker 5, coffee pot 2
Content:
makes a great pot of coffee but getting the coffee out of the pot itself is another matter entirely. Drips, dribbles and splatters. You have to pour it just exactly right - not to fast and not too slow - or you will have it all over the counter. 
The espresso part is great all the way around.
