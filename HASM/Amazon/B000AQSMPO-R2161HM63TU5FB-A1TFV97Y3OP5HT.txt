Title: Keurig B60
Content:
I never seemed to be able to measure coffee properly, so I switched to the one cup type.  The original one cup brewer had a lot of problems.  I got the Keurig B60 and have been delighted.  The coffee is brewed perfectly, and the appliance itself is of high quality manufacture.
