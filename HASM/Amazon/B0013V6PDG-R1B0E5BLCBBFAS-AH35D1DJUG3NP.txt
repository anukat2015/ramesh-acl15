Title: Cold Floors No Longer a Problem
Content:
I spend long hours at my computer and I keep my apartment at around 58 degrees. The floors however are cold and I wanted a heated foot pad or foot rest to warm my feet and legs. The Toasty Toes Heated Footrest fulfilled my needs perfectly; the price was right and the unit is economical to operate.
