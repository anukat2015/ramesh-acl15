Title: Love this heater!
Content:
I searched for this particular heater via the Internet and found that it was likely a discontinued Bionaire item. I was so happy to be able to locate one through Amazon! It fits perfectly on my bathroom counter under the window sill. It's various heat settings are easily controlled using the remote. The 2 sections can be directed to blow heat at different angles for those nippy morning showers. Love it! And what a bargain price!
