Title: Kozy World - what I expected
Content:
I have had this LP heater in operation for a few weeks.  It has definitely served the purpose that I expected.  I have a similar heater made by a different company. When comparing the two, I have to say that this heater has some odor do it whereas the other does not.  It puts out the heat properly and other than the odor, I am completely satisfied.
