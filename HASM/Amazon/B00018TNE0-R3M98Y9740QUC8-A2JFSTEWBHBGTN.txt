Title: still using it 4 years later
Content:
This is a simple and cheap product that works.  It does not turn off automatically, and I always worry I'lll leave it on and burn it out or burn the house down, but I haven't.  I have left it on overnight... oops... but it survived!  It doesn't need paper filters, just rinse out the filter supplied.  Definitely a thumbs up for this product.
