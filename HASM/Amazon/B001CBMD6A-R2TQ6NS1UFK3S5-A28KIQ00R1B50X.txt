Title: Good entry level DSLR, the good pictures are out there, you just have to find them.
Content:
Anyone who buys this camera thinking that this will solve most of your photographic ills is sorely mistaken.  The camera is user friendly and has automatic functions.  If I have a problem with the automatic functions, it would be that it quickly opts to the flash mode in low light settings.
