Title: Great player but...
Content:
This is a great MP3 player. The sound is great. I have been buying Sony products for years and was not disappointed. But good luck trying to find accessories for it.  It does not fit the iPod cases and will not work with speakers for iPods.  I finally found a neoprene case for it but had to look in several stores to find it.
