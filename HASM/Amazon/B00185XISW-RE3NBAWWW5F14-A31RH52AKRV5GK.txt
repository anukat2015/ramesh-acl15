Title: Gives you more than you expect
Content:
I received my eco-heater over a moth ago. I installed it in a 600 square foot guest house hoping it would keep the pipes from freezing this winter.  That is all I wanted.  Well, for the past 2-weeks the night time temps have been around 25 degrees and the coldest the cabin got was 51.8 degrees. Warmest was 68.7 degrees. And my power bill only went up 9 dollars for the month. The only draw back is the pannel gets hotter than reported. I have my fingers crossed and we will see how it holds up when it gets in the single digits ...
