Title: Fantastic coffee, but leaks!
Content:
This coffee pot makes the best coffee of any coffee maker I've ever owned.  But after a while it will leak all over the counter.  I returned it, got another brand and after a while it did the same thing.  I love the coffee from this machine, but I am looking for another coffee maker now.  Any suggestions?
