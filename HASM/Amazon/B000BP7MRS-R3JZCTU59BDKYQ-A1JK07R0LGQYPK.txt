Title: Braun #2
Content:
I bought this product as a replacement for the same coffee maker that lasted at least 9 years.  It makes hot and great tasting coffee each time and it's fast.  The carafe is sturdy with a very comfortable handle and the lid comes off for easy cleaning.  I give this a 'thumbs up' all the way!
