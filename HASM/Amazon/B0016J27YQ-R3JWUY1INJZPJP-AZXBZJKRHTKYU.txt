Title: Excellent for it's size
Content:
This little unit is excellent for it's size! Living in an area where the temps get as high as 114* not many units would help, but this one made all the difference! If you are looking for a unit to help cool a room that doesn't get enough air or you need to cool a 12X12 room this is the perfect unit.
