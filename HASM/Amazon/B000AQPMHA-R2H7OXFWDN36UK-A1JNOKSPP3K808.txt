Title: Best Coffee Maker Ever
Content:
The Keurig is the best coffee maker I have ever owned.  It makes coffee whenever you want it and the selection of kcups is incredible.  You can get the kcups everywhere on-line or in stores.  The coffee is wonderful and exactly the right amount.  You never have to grind the coffee, measure it or adjust to get a great cup of coffee.  No mess either.  Just toss the used kcup and put another one in.
