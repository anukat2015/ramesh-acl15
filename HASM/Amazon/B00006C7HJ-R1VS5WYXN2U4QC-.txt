Title: 3 Stricks, it's out!
Content:
My excitement at getting a top of the line Vornado for my office quickly turned to gloom when the first one sounded like a mac truck was driving through the room.  Back to the store for a replacement only to come home and find that on the second Vornado the fan didn't work.  Once again back to the store, but this time I asked that we try it before I headed home -- only to find the Vornado didn't turn on at all!  Before I could say a word the salesperson recomended I give up and buy something else.  What a waste of time and for a lot of money!
