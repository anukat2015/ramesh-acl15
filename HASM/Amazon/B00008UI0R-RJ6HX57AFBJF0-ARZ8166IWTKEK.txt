Title: Super suction
Content:
This cannister vacuum gets pet hair (3 cats) and it isn't even called a pet hair vacuum. Does a good job on bare floors and an excellent job on carpets, even shag. I would like it to be a little lighter and a little more manuverable, but it's well worth the price. Love the retractable cord.
