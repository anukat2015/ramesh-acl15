Title: Just as good as ever!
Content:
Have only had Bunn coffeemakers for years. Love the looks of the black and stainless. This is the first one I have owned that was not the white B model, so was surprised and pleased to find the drip doesn't start until the lid, on the fill tube, is closed. Love it, love it, love it!!
