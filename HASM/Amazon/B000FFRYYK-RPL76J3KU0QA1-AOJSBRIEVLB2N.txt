Title: Best out there
Content:
This is a simple, reliable machine that makes the best coffee I have ever had at home.  There must be a reason you see Bunns in all of the fine restaraunts.  It may not look real fancy, but this machine has it where it counts.  Fast perfect coffee.  I have not had any issues with the carafe.  I do not foresee ever changing brands.
