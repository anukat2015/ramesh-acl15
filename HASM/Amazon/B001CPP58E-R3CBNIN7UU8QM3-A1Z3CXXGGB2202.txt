Title: Thermostat pretty useless, but otherwise a decent heater
Content:
As far as I can tell the thermostat doesn't really work.  I have the unit under my desk and if I set it to 69 degrees it comes on for a minute and never seems to come back on again.  I have to turn it way up and then turn it on and off as needed.  But that's why I bought a heater with a remote...
