Title: Portable Evaporative Cooler
Content:
This is a great little cooler that will cool down a small to medium size room. It is quiet, small, making it easy to roll around.  There is even a compartment for ice cubes, should you  desire to use them. We used them - just to check it out - and it cooled the room even more.
Overall, it is quiet, efficient, easy to maneuver and affordable.
