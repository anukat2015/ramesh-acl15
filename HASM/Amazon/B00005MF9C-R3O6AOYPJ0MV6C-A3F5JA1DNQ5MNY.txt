Title: Great product for under $20
Content:
I've owned several of these and have gifted many more over the past 10 years.  I love the product.  In fact, I have a cup brewing as I type this review.  It's great for home, the office and road trips.  I'm concerned that it's being sold for over $60 here when I've never paid more than $20 for them in stores.
