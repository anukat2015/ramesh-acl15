Title: Awful Taste!!
Content:
The coffee pods are very expensive, and the coffee is weak and has a really funky taste.  I have ran lots of water through the system, but it didn't help.  I have tried Senseo, Folgers and the Capachino pods and the bad taste is always there. YUCK!!
