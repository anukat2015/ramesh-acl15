Title: On/off switch broke
Content:
Nice vacuum. Easy light but poor quality. The on/off switch broke after 6 months. Also cord is too short.
Closest repair center is at >20 miles. Not worth the effort for a $60 vacuum. Doubting whether to buy the same one again of a more expensive Miele.
