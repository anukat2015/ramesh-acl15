Title: Zojirushi coffee maker
Content:
This is a great coffee maker.  The coffee stays hot for several hours. The thermal carafe is well made. The cone drip chamber helps to extract the all the coffee flavor. It brews quickly.   Three stars becuase the housing is cheap, thin plastic and I am sure something will crack or break off within a year of so.
