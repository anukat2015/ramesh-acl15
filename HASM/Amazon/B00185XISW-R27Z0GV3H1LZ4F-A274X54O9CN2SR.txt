Title: Very large for the amount of heat
Content:
This product works fine but is sure is large for the amount of heat it provides.  I got it to use in a bathroom but when I saw how big it was I could not fit it in that room on the wall.  It gets very hot so you will not want to bump into it with bare skin.  Probably not good to have around with small kids.
