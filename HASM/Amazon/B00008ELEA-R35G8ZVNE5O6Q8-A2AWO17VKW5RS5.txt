Title: 8 cup Millennium
Content:
The Farberware Millennium superfast coffee pot makes the best coffee. I have owned several other coffee makers and coffee pots and my 8 cup Millennium makes fast good tasting hot coffee. I am very pleased with my purchase. The pot also has an added bonus, it does not take up much space on your counter top and it is more pleasing to look at than a big, tall, ugly, fat coffee maker.
