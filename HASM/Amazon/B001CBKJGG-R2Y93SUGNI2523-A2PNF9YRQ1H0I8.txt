Title: Canon Rebel XS 10.1MP Digital is excellent at a reasonable price
Content:
This camera was purchased as a gift for my wife.  She has enjoyed it very much and was able to quickly learn how to take very good pictures.  It came with an EF-S 18-55mm f/3.5-5.6 IS Lens which seems to be a very versatile lens for overall family picture taking and ease of use. Grandchildren and great grandchildren have already been photographed for our enjoyment.
