Title: Ideal Bathroom heater
Content:
I purchased one of these earlier in the year for use in my bathroom, and was so pleased with the result, that I purchased this second unit for my other bathroom.  I only use it in an on/off mode for 10 to 15 minutes to take the chill out of the air when I'm going to take a shower, so I really can't comment on temperature stability in the thermostat operating mode.
