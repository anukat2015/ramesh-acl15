Title: Great personal coffeemaker
Content:
Since my husband and I are not big coffee drinkers, I never make a full pot.  This coffeemaker allows us to make one cup at a time whenever we want it.  It's always fresh, hot, and quick.  I have bought several for gifts for friends who want one for their desk at work.
