Title: Great little Dirt Devil
Content:
Great little machine!  Powerful, lightweight and small, which I love.  For me it's important that it's bagless and so easy to empty.  The accessories allow you to clean out all the corners and little spaces effectively.  I do love a canister system;  they're so much more efficient than those heavy uprights.
