Title: Picks up pet hair GREAT - Definitely recommend
Content:
I have two American bulldogs (they have short hair) that shed and it sticks on my couch.  I've tried many different brushes and lint removers, but this product truly delivers.  In a matter of minutes, my couch was virtually pet hair free.  It is extremely powerful; the only thing I don't like is the motor gets pretty hot.
