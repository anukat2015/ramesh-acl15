Title: Wonderful product
Content:
I purchased this mp3 player as a gift for a friend.  My friend is not computer savvy at all but wanted a device he could put listen to books on when he traveled.  I knew it had to be something simple to operate or he would never use it.  He is able to drag and drop his songs/books from his computer to the mp3 player with ease.  It was also easy find the folder with the correct files when you wanted to listen to a certain book or song. I am very pleased with this purchase because my friend is so happy with his gift.
