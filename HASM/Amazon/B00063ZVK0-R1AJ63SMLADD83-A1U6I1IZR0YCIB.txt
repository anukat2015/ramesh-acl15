Title: Excellent Little Machine
Content:
This is a powerful and safe little machine.  It's the 2nd one I have bought so I can use it in two rooms of my house.  Great for pre-heating the bathroom before stepping out of the shower or bath.  Great for working in the drafty basement or even for chilly office space at work.  We've even used it in the fall out on our screened patio to sit out for drinks after dinner when it's just a little nippy.  Best little heater I've ever had!  Several friends have seen it in action at my house and bought their own.  I highly recommend this item.
