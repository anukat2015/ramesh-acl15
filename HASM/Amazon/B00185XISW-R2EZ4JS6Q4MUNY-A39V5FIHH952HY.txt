Title: This is a toasty product in the best sense of the word.
Content:
I love this little wall heater.  It's unobtrusive, quiet, efficient, and IT WORKS perfectly.  It's on or it's off.  The room toasts up nicely.  I couldn't be happier.  Wish I'd had one years and years ago.  It will not heat a whole house.  It is room specific, but worth the money.
