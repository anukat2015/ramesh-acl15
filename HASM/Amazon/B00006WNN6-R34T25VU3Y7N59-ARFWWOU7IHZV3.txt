Title: Great office warmer...
Content:
Perfect to take the chill off a never quite warm enough office.  This unit is quiet, small and does the job.  Can't beat the price either.  Only cons are temp & fan adjustments aren't strikingly obvious, and the light stays on even when unit is off.
