Title: Mr Heater 12 Ft Hose Assembly
Content:
The hose works fine with the Little Buddy heater.  The black plastic star nut was loose in the package, and it was a bit awkward to get the hose with its brass fitting held in a vise so the plastic star could be tapped on.  Otherwise, no problems.
