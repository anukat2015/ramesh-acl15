Title: A safe and reliable heater
Content:
Highly recommend this unit; has a timer that can be used to turn the unit on and off on half hour intervals.  It has sealed oil filled tubing that keeps warm after the unit is turned off. Great to keep a fairly large room at a cozy temperature, and a must for those who can not take the cold.
