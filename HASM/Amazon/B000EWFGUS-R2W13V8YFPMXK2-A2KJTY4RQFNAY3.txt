Title: Hamilton Beach 43253 Ensemble 12-Cup Coffeemaker, Red
Content:
When looking for a Coffee Maker we wanted one that would not leak water, or drip on the warmer plate plus easy to fill and make good coffee, and we definitely found one in this Coffee maker.  Not only is it pretty but it does not drip water on the warmer plate as others have but also easy to fill plus makes excellent coffee.  Highly recommend.
