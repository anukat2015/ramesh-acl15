Title: The Best Coffee Maker
Content:
I have had several other brands of coffee makers and Braun is by far the best. The coffee tastes great and they last forever.  You can't beat the price either.  I also love how they are small and not bulky on the counter.  The built in filter (replaceable) that you can put in them work great to make the water taste great too.  I love this maker!
