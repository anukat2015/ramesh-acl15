Title: This little coffee maker rocks!
Content:
The Zelco Brisk Brew Travel Coffeemaker is perfect for the frequent traveler.  It's small, lightweight, and brews a great cup of coffee. I'm a traveling musician, and take mine with me everywhere.  I never have to worry about whether or not a hotel has a coffee maker, or what kind of coffee I'll be ingesting. I love this little brewer!
