Title: Bunn Bunn Bunn
Content:
I keep hearing all the buzz about Bunn coffee makers, I think if that's what you want ...then buy it..your not gonna think anything tastes good compared to a Bunn! We LOVE this coffee maker and the price could not be beat as far as we are concerned...the machine performs at a medium sound level, brews HOT coffee and has a temp setting, cleans nice and was a whole lot less than your Bunn! If your hesitant..don't be..it's well worth it. Mike
