Title: Extremely nice item, absolutely worth the money!  Cold killer...
Content:
It's a nice, cheap & compact device to be used for killing the cold! You don't even have to keep it on for a long time, just use it for lil while & it will make a small room like mine warm. I cannot use my home's heater as much because of electric bill & my parents so purchased this solution which pretty much covered me.
