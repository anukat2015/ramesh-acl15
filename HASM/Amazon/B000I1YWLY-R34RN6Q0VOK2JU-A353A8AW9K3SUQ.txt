Title: Great!
Content:
It works great!  Very light weight which is what I was looking for, I like the ease of changing from carpet to bare floor with a push of a button.  It is very manageable around furniture and under furniture.  Highly recommended, with three cats and one big yellow dog it picks up lots of hair, I have to empty it often but expected that with all the hair.  Also the shipping was fast, and well packaged!
