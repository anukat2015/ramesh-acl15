Title: Bubble Gum Machine Vacuum
Content:
Great vacuum if you don't have carpet in your house.  This thing has ok suction, but will not suck up anything off carpet.  It depends upon only it's suction to vacuum capet as it has no rotating brush.
I bought it this morning and it's already back at the store.  I wouldn't buy it for $5 more or less $60-something.
