Title: Grind & Brew Cusinart
Content:
I have the black version. It was great for the first three months and then it became almost impossible to remove the grinder to clean it! It gets stuck in place so tight that I had to use plyers to get it out and clean it up so that I could send it back to the company for a new one! Hope this one works better.
