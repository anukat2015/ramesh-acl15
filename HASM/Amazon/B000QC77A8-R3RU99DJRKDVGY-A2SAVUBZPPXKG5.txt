Title: Just ok
Content:
Last year,we had one of these in a small (10X10) out building. It was ok. It kept the room kinda warm even on cold nights. Problem is,it doesn't keep your feet warm. Had to get another small heater that would heat my feet and I'm not usually one to get cold easily.
