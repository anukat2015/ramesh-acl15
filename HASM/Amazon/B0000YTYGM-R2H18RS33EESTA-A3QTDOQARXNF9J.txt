Title: Good overall
Content:
This automatic coffee maker works as advertised.  The thermal carafe keeps coffee hot for several hours.  The basket is easily removed for washing after the coffee's brewed.  However, the display space that shows the time is very small.  And it is quite difficult to discern whether the "auto" feature is on or off.
