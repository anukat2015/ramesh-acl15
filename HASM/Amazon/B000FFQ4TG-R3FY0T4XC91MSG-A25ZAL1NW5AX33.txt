Title: It's a Bunn
Content:
I've had this coffee maker for several months, it's the third Bunn product I've owned and I wouldn't buy any other coffee maker. I don't like the revolving filler lid as well as the flip up lid on my previous model, but I like the looks of it and it makes a great cup of coffee.
