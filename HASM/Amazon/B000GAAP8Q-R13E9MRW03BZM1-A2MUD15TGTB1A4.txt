Title: From Sony to iPod
Content:
I used to own a Sony NW-E507. The only advantage of that Sony was the battery life, iPod is far away from it. iPod looks great and stylish, is pretty lightweight and thin, iTunes is much more easier to work with than SonicStage, searching capability is an unresistable option, all the other options are fascinating and might come handy.
