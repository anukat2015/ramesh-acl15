Title: Under my desk at work
Content:
i put this little heater under my desk at work, and it's PERFECT.  I have to have it right next to my feet... so it wouldn't be ideal for a larger space.  But I'm usually freezing in the office, but not with this little guy.  It's also, basically, SILENT.  So it's not disturbing in the workplace at all.  I'd recommend it for people needing a heater at work.
