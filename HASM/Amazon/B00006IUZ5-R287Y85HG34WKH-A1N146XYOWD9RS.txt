Title: Great Buy
Content:
This is the 2nd product of this type I have purchased. One for my home and one for Camp. When I make tea or coffee, I have only one cup, and don't have to wait forever for the water to boil on the stove.  It is also great for heating water for Instant Oatmeal.  This is just so convienient. I love it. I would recommend this product to every one.
