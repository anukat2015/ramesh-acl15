Title: I LOVE this gadget!
Content:
I received the latte maker for Christmas and have already used it a dozen times.  I have had no problems cleaning it.  The unit comes with a recipe book with some great hot drink ideas.  I am working my way through them all.  My husband hopes this will cut down on my monthly Starbucks spending!  The latte maker is easy to use and easy to clean.  Great gift idea for the lazy coffee maker in your life.
