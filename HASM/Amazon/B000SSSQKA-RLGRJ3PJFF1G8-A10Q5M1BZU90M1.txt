Title: Good product
Content:
This vacuum as simple as it is, has a very strong vacuum performance, and although the 3-in-1 brush does not have a powered rotating brush, it works fine.
It is not that light, as I would have preferred, but it is still lighter than some of the canister models out there.
For the price, this is a good buy.
