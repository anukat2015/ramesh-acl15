Title: I love BUNN coffee
Content:
This is the best coffee maker ever!  I haven't had any problems with the pot dribbling like some other reviewers.  It brews perfect coffee, keeps it hot for hours, and it's done in a few minutes, which is great (especially if you knew how impatient I am for my coffee in the morning!).
