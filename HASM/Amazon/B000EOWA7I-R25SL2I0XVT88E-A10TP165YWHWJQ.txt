Title: Good for the price
Content:
At first I thought I'd be sending this back because it didn't seem to suck up the dirt too well,but then I tried it out with the attachments and it worked so much better.I don't use it without them on.I use the upholstery attachment for the carpet & the crevice one works great for all those,well,crevices!I wouldn't say it has super sucking power,but it does the job and it was a great deal!!
