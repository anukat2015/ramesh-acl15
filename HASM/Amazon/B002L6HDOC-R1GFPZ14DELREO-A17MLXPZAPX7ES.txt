Title: Delighted with new nano
Content:
I bought this nano for my husband for his birthday. He was delighted with the additional features like the FM radio tuner and the video capability. He was able to plug the iPod directly into his Xbox and show the videos on our living room tv, which we thought was neat. I was impressed with the big, bright, colorful screen, such an improvement over my older nano.
