Title: Should have switched a long time ago
Content:
Mac is the Cadillac of computers. It looks good and works just as good as it looks. Very fast, amazing display, user friendly and lots of extras built in. Now that I am able to run Windows and Mac on the same machine, I find I am using Windows less and less.
