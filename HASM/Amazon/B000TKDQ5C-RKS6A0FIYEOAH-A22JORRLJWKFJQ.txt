Title: Lasko 754200 ceramic heater
Content:
The heater is small but has an adjustable thermostat and settings of 900 & 1500 watts. My computer room is small, so I close the door and the heater warms it up nicely. Price was good, heater does as expected. Noise level is fine with me. Not too loud.
