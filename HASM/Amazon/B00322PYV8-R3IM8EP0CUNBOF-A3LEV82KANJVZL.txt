Title: SONY VAIO PERFECTION!!!
Content:
After years of having almost every brand on the market except Sony I was anxious to give it a shot. I first checked out BBs but seeing how much I could save by going with this Amazon version I was in. This Sony Vaio does it all. My favorite is the light up keyboard. Makes life easier when its later in the evening and I want to go online. Blu ray playback also comes in handy. This is hands down the best laptop I have owned to date!!!
