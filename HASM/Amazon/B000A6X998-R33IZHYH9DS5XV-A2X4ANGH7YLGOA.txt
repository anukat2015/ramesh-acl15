Title: Great Floor Heater!
Content:
My husband recently retired and is actively working in his garage.  As a civil engineer he had this product in his trailer, and welcomed its heat during the cold, rainy winter days.  I surprised him by getting this for his garage to keep him warm on those chilly days.  The heater is very user friendly, easy to turn on and off.  I would truly recommend this product.
