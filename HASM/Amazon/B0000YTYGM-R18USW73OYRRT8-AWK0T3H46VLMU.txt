Title: The best office pot EVER!
Content:
This is the best coffee pot I have ever used!  We have some hard core coffee drinkers at our office and this insulated pot keeps coffee hot for HOURS!  I am usually the first person in each morning and take the job of making the first pot of coffee.  Most mornings there is still coffee from the day before and it is still hot - not luke warm...HOT.  This is a great purchase for those who like to drink their coffee all day long.
