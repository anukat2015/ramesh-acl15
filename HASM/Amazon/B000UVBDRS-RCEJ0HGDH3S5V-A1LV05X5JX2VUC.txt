Title: a nice mp3 player
Content:
This is a nice mp3 player, easy to use, and the screen is pretty good. However, the earphones provided with the player is not so good. I changed a SONY earphone, and the sound effects are much better than the original one. Just take consider to change with an earphone around $20, it would be a nice choice.
