Title: Toasty bathroom
Content:
Our master bathroom is quite cold, so we decided to purchase a small heater. We were pleased with this product. It is quiet and in about 10 min. our large bathroom was adequately warmed. We would recommend this product. If it is placed close to your body (like near a desk) it gets hot quickly.
