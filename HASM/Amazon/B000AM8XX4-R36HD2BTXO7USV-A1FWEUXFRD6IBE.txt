Title: Great Buy!!
Content:
I love this little MP3 player.  It was all that I was looking for in an MP3 player and it was inexpensive compared to other players like it.  It has nice features and design.  The battery life is great and only needs one AAA.  The only downside is that I'm afriad it might break the first time I drop it.  It just doesn't feel very durable.
