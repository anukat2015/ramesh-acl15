Title: DO NOT ORDER THIS PRODUCT
Content:
First, I have other DeLonghi products and they're great. This one might be, too, but if you buy it from Amazon, it'll arrive damaged. They ship it in the original packaging; this heavy piece of electronics gets bounced around enough to shatter plastic. My advice is trust Amazon with books, CDs and DVDs, but NOT big electronics.
