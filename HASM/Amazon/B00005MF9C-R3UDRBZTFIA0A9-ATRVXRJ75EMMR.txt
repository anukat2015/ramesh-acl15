Title: handy for tea also!
Content:
If you start with a new machine that has never made coffee, you can use this item to make a nice cup of tea.  It is impossible to remove the coffee oils once they have settled into the cup.  I do wish additional cups were available for this produce as the cups are very handy with nice tight tids.
