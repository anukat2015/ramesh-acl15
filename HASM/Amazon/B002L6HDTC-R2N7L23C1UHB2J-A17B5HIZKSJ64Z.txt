Title: iPod Nano 8gb Green
Content:
This was a purchase to replace an old iPod Shuffle. The learning curve for the Nano is not difficult and in many ways, rather intuitive. I have not yet fully used all the features, but the ability to have a greater anount of music combined with the bonus feature of being video-enabled makes it a great purchase. Highly recommended.
