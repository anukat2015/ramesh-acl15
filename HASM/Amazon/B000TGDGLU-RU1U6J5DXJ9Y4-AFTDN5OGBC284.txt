Title: Warm winter with this product
Content:
I'm satisfied with this. I needed heating machine at home and I bought this, because as many people pointed out that it has many advantages, such as safety and oxygen burning free. This machine is smaller than I expected but produces a lot of heat enough to fill my room. This product might not be for large space such as living room.
Usage is simple enough, however, I don't use comport temperature feature because it looks confusing to me.
