Title: The beat space heater I ever used
Content:
We are using the heater in our bedroom. It has all the features we wanted. It occupies a small space, is quite, has a remote control and rotates.
It enables us to keep the overall temperature of the house low while enjoying a warmer bedroom.
I would recommend it without reservation.
