Title: so simple, so perfect, so cheap!!
Content:
the water boils super fast and it makes good coffee. the filter is already inside so you don't even have to buy any! it comes with the tumbler - which sadly doesn't have a close-able seal. (there is an opening, but you can't slide it shut) it's small and it's perfect for people who drink a cup or two a day. easy to clean!
