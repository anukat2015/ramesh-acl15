Title: Mr. Coffee Frappe Maker
Content:
I've had the Frappe Maker for a month and use it everyday.  I have read reviews that it sometimes goes all over and that has happened to me a few times, but I do not know why.  I now hold a paper towel around it when it starts to blink just to be safe.  I love it and wouldn't want to be without it, I am addicted to Frappes.
