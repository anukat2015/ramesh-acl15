Title: 3 years and no problems
Content:
We've had this coffee maker for three years and have never had an issue with it. We use the auto on feature 5 days a week -- it turns on, makes coffee and keeps it warm. 
  
 Never had a problem with spillage, pouring et cetera in all that time.
