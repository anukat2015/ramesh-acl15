Title: A Must Have
Content:
My first Bunn lasted for over 17 years. We purchased this new one and the best feature is the lid over the water reservoir slides. If it is open no water flows into the filter unit and pot. This unit is ideal for storing under the wall cabinets.  It doesn't take up much space either. And always, Bunn is top quality. Don't waste your money on seconds every six months. Get the Bunn.
