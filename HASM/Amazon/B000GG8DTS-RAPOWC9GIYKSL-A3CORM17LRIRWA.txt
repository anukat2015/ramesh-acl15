Title: Space Heater
Content:
I thought this might be a wee bit bigger than it is, but it is still a decent heater for the money.  I use it in my bathroom on the chilly mornings.  Kicks off some heat!  My dogs also like it.....they congregate in front of it on cold days.  I like that it is quiet, as well.
