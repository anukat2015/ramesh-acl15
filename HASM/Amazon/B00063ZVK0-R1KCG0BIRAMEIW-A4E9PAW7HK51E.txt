Title: A Touch of Warmth
Content:
This heater was perfect for what we needed it for: adding supplemental heat to our first floor living space in a 120 year old house.  The exterior of the unit stays nice and cool, so it can be easily moved around if needed.  The warmth is strongly circulated by the fan.  I heartily recommend this heater.
