Title: Nice coffeemaker
Content:
I think Braun did a good job in the design and functionality of the KF580 coffeemaker. I like the adjustable shutoff timer and the balance when pouring coffee.  Coffee temperature is just right.  The biggest complaint is that the unit was hard to find!
