Title: Simply Awesome
Content:
I got the nano for my 7 year old son as a replacement for his MP3 player. This product is so much easier to use than his other unit. Not only is it attractive but, the functionality sets it apart from its competitors. In particular, my son loves the video function and has created many videos with his sister and friends.
