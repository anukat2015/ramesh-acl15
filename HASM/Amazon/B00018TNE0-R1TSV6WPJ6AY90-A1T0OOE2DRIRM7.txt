Title: One Month
Content:
We bought the one cup coffee maker as my husband wants one cup in the morning.  We tried this coffee maker as there are not too many one cup coffee makers that are not the gourmet type.  It worked for less than one month.  The water stopped flowing through the coffee.  I would not recommend it.
