Title: Nice MP3 player, but...
Content:
needs a case to protect the player against falls, Coby should produce their on case because I've been at several stores and haven't been able to find one that fits this player. The earphones are way too big, they should be provided in a smaller size. Other than that the player is excellent for my 10 year old boy, he is pretty happy with it.
