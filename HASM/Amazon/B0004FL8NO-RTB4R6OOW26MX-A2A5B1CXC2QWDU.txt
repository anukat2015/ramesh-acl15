Title: Great coffee maker
Content:
I love this coffee maker. No more burnt coffee sitting on a hot plate. The thermal pot keeps the coffee hot for hours. Hot enough for me anyway. I really like the black/stainless look. It's fast brewing as well. My only concern is that the lid to the water tank seems a bit flimsy, but time will tell.
