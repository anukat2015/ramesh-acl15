Title: Great product, Great Price
Content:
This model heater turned up tops on many lists as I was researching all makes and models of space heaters for my basement.  The options were great and the price reasonable.
I've had it now for a few weeks and it works very well, it's quiet, and no shock on the electric bill yet.  All and all a great investment and I highly recommend it.
