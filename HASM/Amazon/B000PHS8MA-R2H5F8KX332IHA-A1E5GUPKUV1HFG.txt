Title: A Great MP3 Player
Content:
The Creative Zen Stone 1 GB MP3 player is a great little device.  It's simple to use, tiny and has crystal clear sound quality.  I also use it to listen to audio books and it is the easiest audio book player I've ever used. I would highly recommend this product.  The downside is the price of the accessories.  I think they could be more reasonably priced.  Other than that, buy one, you'll love it!
