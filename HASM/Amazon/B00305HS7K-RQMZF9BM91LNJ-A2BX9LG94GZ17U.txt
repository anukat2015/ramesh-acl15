Title: This thing is a beast
Content:
Just got my Qosmio, and I am absolutely floored by this thing.  I have been a long time supported of Toshiba, and this product only further confirms my love for them.  However, I just wanted to clear this up for everyone.  This listing says that there is a 2.8ghz processor in this, when it is actually a 1.6.  Just wanted to clear that up for everyone.  Don't let that stop you from buying this thing.  It will amaze you, inside and out.  Have fun!
