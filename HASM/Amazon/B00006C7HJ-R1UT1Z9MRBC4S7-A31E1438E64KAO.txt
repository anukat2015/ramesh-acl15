Title: Works great
Content:
I have one and I bought two as presents. At first, people seem to think that it's another 'space heater'. Ho hum. Then once they try it, they are amazed that it works so well. Mine is turned on at this very minute. I have to shut it off after 10 minute because the room gets pretty warm. Would recommend this in a hearbeat. Not cheap but worth it.
