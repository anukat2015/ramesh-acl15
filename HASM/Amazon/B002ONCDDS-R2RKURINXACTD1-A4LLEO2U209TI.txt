Title: Perfect 14inch laptop!
Content:
The HP Pavilion DV4-1541US 14.1-Inch is a perfect blend of performance, style, design and price. It is just about 5lbs so pretty light -great for webcam sessions, music, movies and general web surfing. The Core 2 duo processor and the 4GB RAM make this a very performant machine. I cannot think of a better laptop for this price which is as packed with features - Highly Satisfied!
