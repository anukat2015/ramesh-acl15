Title: Buy Two!
Content:
I had to buy two of these coffee makers for Mom this Christmas.  She wears them out over the years and wanted one for her home and one when she visits Grandma.  If you like perked coffee, this is a great coffee pot for you.  Fast, easy to clean, and stays hot without that "boiled" coffee taste.
