Title: HP TouchSmart TX2-1270US 12.1-Inch Laptop, i feel cheated from the false advertising @ Amazon
Content:
Hp made a good system, the machine is doing all I expected it to do. I only have one complaint and that is the false advertising from Amazon.com. I double checked the system before purchase and ensured a s-video port was installed from the zoomed product images as of this date still being displayed on there order site for the exact same price. However, this feature was not installed on my system which was part of my main reason for purchase. The future does not look good for shopping for electronics on Amazon.com again, if what they display is not what they are selling!!
