Title: Best Vacuum to purchase
Content:
Like this vacuum so much that I bought one for each of my daughters.  Great suction, no bags to buy, stows away - and canisters are best design - uprights can't get under furniture and are heavy.  Dysons are expensive but really get stuff clean!  I will never go back.
