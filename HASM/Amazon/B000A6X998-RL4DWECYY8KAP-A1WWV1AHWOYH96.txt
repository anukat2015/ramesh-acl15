Title: excellent heater
Content:
this heater does an excellent job, it is easy to use and thus far quite reliable. the only design changes that would be helpful, would be a slightly altered hand hold for moving it from place to place, and an adjustment to the on off button so that it could be shut off by pressing an on off button separately from the thermostat button.
