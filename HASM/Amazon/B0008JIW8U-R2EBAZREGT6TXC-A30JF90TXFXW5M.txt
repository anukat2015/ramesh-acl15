Title: DRX5 Programmable 4 cup Mr. Coffee
Content:
Very happy with my 4 cup Mr. Coffee maker.  It's just the right size for two of us to have our fresh morning cup of coffee and we perk another pot in the evening to have a fresh cup with our dinner.  We're not habitual coffee drinkers and we don't like coffee reheated or sitting in the pot all day...we like it fresh, fresh, fresh.  This is perfect for us.  Thank you!
