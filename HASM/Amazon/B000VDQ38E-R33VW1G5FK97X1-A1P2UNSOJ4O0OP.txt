Title: Great for pet hair!
Content:
I bought this vacuum based on reviews I had read.  They are right!  It picks up dog hair with ease -- much better than other vacuums.  It also seems to pick up a lot more dirt as well.  So far, it is one of the best vacuums I have owned.
