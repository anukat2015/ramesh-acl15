Title: Great Vac!
Content:
I love my vac - it is very powerful. I can reach the ceiling and windows with the hose. However, the vac blows hot air out of the back. So you need to be careful not to point the vac in a location that hasn't been cleaned yet - otherwise you'll be blowing dust into the air.
