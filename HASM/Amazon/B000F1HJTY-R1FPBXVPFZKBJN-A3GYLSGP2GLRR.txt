Title: Nextar mp3
Content:
This is a very handy and easy to use tool. Just plug it directly to you USB port and download, erase, and reset, whatever you want. 
The battery is a AAA that is easy to find and extremely easy to replace. 
It has no belt clip but a small cell phone holder from the dollar store works fine.
