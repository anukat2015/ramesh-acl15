Title: Coffee Isn't Hot Enough
Content:
I like the product; it looks good and makes great tasting coffee.  I am disappointed that the coffee initially is hot, but cools too quickly.  I find myself having to microwave the coffee to have it as hot as I like it.  The coffee also pours very slowly.
