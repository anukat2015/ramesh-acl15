Title: Lenovo Y-550 review
Content:
I am very happy with my purchase, this laptop PC is very fast, the battery lasts as long as advertised, the LED display is very bright and yet an energy saver. The keyboard honors the IBM legacy, it is very comfortable to use. The sound of the built in speakers is crystal clear. All in all I highly recommend this product.
