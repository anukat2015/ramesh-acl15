Title: AWESOME
Content:
Our small office has been looking for a good coffee maker for years - most of them would burn, and the cup-at-a-time maker we bought made poorly-tasting coffee. This one works great. The resevoir keeps enough coffee hot for a small office for hours - with a fresh taste. Easy to clean and fill. Works great.
