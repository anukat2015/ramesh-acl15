Title: Nice one-cup coffee maker
Content:
Coffee is great, but there are not enough choices of different coffees.  The K cup has a great selection and for that reason, I am sorry we didn't get a coffee maker that uses K cups.  Also, you can't use your own coffee - you must use Nescafe pods.
