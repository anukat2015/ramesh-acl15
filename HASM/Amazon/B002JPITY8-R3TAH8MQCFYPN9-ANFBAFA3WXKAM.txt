Title: Amazing little player
Content:
PROS
*ZunePass download all the music you want for $15/month, keep 10 songs per month DRM-Free)
*720p HDMI output
*Awesome PC software. Unlike iTunes this thing is Fast and a pleasure to use.
*Good looking device, def has the Wow factor
*OLED screen (brighter, more vibrant than LCD)
*Free HD radio
*XBOX Live integration
*nVidia Tegra Chip (great graphics, some good looking games are free to download)
*On-demand music (play music online with your Zune HD, don't need to download it to your hard drive) 
CONS
*Needs more Apps
*A viable SDK is needed for dev and a proper way for devs to distribute their apps. It currently has only XNA support.
*Needs to support more Hidef formats like MKV
