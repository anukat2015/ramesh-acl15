Title: iPod is still the best
Content:
Bought this new iPod to replace my 16 year old daughter's old iPod & she couldn't be happier.  The new design of the 3rd generation really makes it more user friendly & appealing!  Like I said, iPod is still the best MP3 player out there & I won't buy anything else.
