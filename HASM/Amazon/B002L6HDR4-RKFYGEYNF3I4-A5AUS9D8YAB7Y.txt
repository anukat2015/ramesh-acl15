Title: I Love My iPod!
Content:
I love my iPod!.  At first I was hesitant to purchase an iPod.  Now that I have it, it's the best purchase that I've made.  I'm so amazed how one could design such a sleek, paper thin piece of technology for every one to enjoy.  Also, I take the iPod with me when I go out to walk and jog.  Having an iPod makes exercising so much more enjoyable and fun.
