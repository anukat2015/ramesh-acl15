Title: very pleased w/ vaccum!
Content:
All I can say is that I love this Shark Roadster!  I have two little dogs who shed like crazy, and when I first brought it home and used it, I couldn't believe the amount of dust, dirt and hair it collected for such a small, compact size.  I was shocked to see how much our regular full-size vaccum was unable to pick up!  Definitely worth the buy!!
