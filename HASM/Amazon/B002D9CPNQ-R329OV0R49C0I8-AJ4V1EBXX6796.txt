Title: Boofo Computer
Content:
I have had this machine for a week and I am totally impressed. The battery life is as advertised. 
One of my prime requirements for a new laptop was a numeric keypad and this has a great one. Still getting used to the placement of the arrow keys. The on/off of the touch pad is the best I have encountered.
