Title: Wonderful heater
Content:
This heater is great!  I set the temp at 70 degrees while I'm gone...come home and my bedroom is the perfect temperature.  It's also very quiet so that when it kicks on while I'm sleeping, it never wakes me up.  I love that it comes with a remote so that I can change the temperature while I'm in bed.  No complaints...this heater is lovely!
