Title: B&D coffe maker
Content:
Bought this coffeemaker to replace one from a different manufacturer that had been recalled as a fire hazard. This product has every feature I wanted and then some, it is also an atractive appliance. The price was a steal for the features and my experiences with Black & Decker have always been positive.
