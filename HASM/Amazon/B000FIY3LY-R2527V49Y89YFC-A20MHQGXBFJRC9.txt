Title: Great heater
Content:
The heater part of this product works great (that's really what I got it for).  Haven't needed the air conditioner yet.  The unit heats a building that is 280 sq. feet.  the outside of the unit stays cool to the touch.  I love the timer!
I would recommend this product.
