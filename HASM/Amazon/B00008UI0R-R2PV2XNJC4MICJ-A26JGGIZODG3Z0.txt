Title: Great Suction Power & Ease Of Use
Content:
I bought this product due to the great reviews it received, and I was NOT disappointed!  It really cleans the floors well and you are able to increase the suction power when needed.  The cord draws in easily with a touch of a lever, and the canister is easy to empty.  It's easy to manuever and clean stairs, etc. This is a well made product!
