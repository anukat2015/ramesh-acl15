Title: Solid Performance
Content:
The product functions very well and provides great value for the price you pay. It heats up small and big rooms very effectively and is ideal for someone who feels that the AC in one's office/home is too cold. It is also ideal for someone who wants to save up on heating costs and for use in colder temperatures up to 0 def F.
