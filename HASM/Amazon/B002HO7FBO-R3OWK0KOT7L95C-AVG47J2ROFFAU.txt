Title: Simple to use and great coffee
Content:
Just purchased this unit and love it.  My wife and I do not drink pots of coffee a day so being able to quickly brew a single great cup of coffee is awesome.   We are not throwing away our old coffee pot so we can brew more when friends come over.  Brewing tons of coffee could get expensive.
