Title: Slow and Hungry
Content:
In my case I got pretty disappointed with this product. The system is quite slow and the battery finishes in less than 2 hours, much different than the promise. I think I will have to add extra memory and live with its minimum autonomy. I wouldn't recommend anyone buying this product with this configuration.
