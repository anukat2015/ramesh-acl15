Title: Trust this brand name after 20 years still working heater
Content:
I have a Delonghi oil filled heater that I purchased 20 years ago. I use it each year.  After 20 years use, it still working flawlessly.  Quiet, produce heat, turn itself on and off based on the heat setting. The relay is also very quiet during switch on and off.
