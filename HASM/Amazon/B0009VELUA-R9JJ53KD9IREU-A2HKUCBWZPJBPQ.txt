Title: I Love This Coffee Maker
Content:
I'll keep it short... It works great, the coffe tastes great, and the coffe stays "fresher" longer than any pot I've ever had. Even after 4 hours it still tastes like a new brew.
My mother-in-law also liked it so much she got one for herself.
