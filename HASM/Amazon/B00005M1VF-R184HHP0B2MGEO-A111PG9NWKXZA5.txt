Title: Great one or two cup coffee maker
Content:
This is a simple to use one or two cup coffee maker that does a good job.
Adjust the amount of coffee you place in it according to the size of your cup. The travel mugs supplied with the machine are larger than a standard coffee cup by about two times so double the coffee grounds placed in the filter over that recomended on the coffee container. Also use your cup you will be drinking from to place the water in the machine to avoid over flowing or less than full cups.
