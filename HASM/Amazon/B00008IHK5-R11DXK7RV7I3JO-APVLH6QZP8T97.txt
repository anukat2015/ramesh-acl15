Title: Great Coffee Maker!
Content:
This is one of the best coffee makers I've ever used.  Cuisinart didn't overlook any features on this one - strength setting, hold-temperature setting, water filter, non-drip carafe, reusable coffee filter, clock, auto-on/off setting, etc.  Makes a great gift as well.
