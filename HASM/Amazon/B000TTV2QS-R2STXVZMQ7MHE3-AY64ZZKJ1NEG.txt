Title: best heater I ever found
Content:
I bought 3 heaters this winter, and finally keep this one. It heats the room pretty fast and not so noisy as others. I only use it in my bedroom, my bedroom is not big and I hanged the heavy curtain. I can use this heater alone at night without turn on the whole house's heater. So far pretty satisfied with this heater.
