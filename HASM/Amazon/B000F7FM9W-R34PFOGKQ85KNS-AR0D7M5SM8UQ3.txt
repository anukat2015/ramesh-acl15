Title: Great design, does not work
Content:
The Elite Cuisine coffemaker is a sleek, modern design, but it does not function.  As read in other reviews (should have heeded the advice), the water is not heated sufficiently to effectively brew coffee.   Tried it several times, same result: warm brown water, with almost no coffee taste.  Returned.
