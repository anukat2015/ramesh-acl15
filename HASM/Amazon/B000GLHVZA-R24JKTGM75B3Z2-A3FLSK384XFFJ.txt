Title: Quiet and effective
Content:
Bought to replace an old gas heater for my parent's kitchen.  It works great.  The only reason I did not give it 5 stars is that I don't believe that it heats as much area as claimed.  The kitchen is small and though the heater will warm the room, it takes a long time.
