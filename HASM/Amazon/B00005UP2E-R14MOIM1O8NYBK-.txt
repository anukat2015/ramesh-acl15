Title: CAN'T CLEAN CARPET!!!!
Content:
THIS VACUUM CANNOT CLEAN CARPETS AT ALL. I HAVE TO SHORTEN THE HOSE AND GO ON MY HANDS AND KNEES TO HAVE IT PICK UP ANYTHING.  I HAVE TO GO OVER EACH AREA AT LEAST 10 TIMES.  THE ONLY POSITIVE THING I COULD SAY IS THAT USING THIS VACUUM GIVES ME A GOOD WORKOUT.  IT DOES A GOOD JOB WITH FLOOR, BUT THE POWER HEAD FOR CARPET IS TERRIBLE!!! I JUST GOT IT A MONTH AGO AND I FIND MYSELF SHOPPING AGAIN FOR A NEW VACUUM.
