Title: Best Devil
Content:
Picks up everything not stuck to the floor or carpet for that matter.  Good capacity for holding dirt, but a little tricky to get the dust bin out of the unit.  Sweeper does "float" accross the floor.  Not as quiet as I would like, but a lot quieter than the hand held units I've tested.
