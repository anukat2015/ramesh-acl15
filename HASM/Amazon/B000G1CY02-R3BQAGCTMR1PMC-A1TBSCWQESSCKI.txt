Title: It's still working during it's 3rd Winter!
Content:
I bought this 2 winters ago and I love it. Silent operation. Heats small to medium rooms well. The only drawback, and I consider this a minor one, is it takes a little while to heat up, but once it's at temp it maintains it well.
