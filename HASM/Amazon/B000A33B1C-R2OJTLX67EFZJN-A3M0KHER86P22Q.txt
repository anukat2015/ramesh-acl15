Title: Delonghi TRD0715T Safe Heat Oil-Filled Radiator
Content:
Great product!  The unit is sealed so that you do not have to add any oil.  Additionally, it has a timer.  I cannot control the temperature in my own office which is often cold.  I have the ability to set the timer so that it can start warming up my office space before I arrive to work.  I highly recommend this heating unit!
