Title: Not good for Napster To Go subscribers
Content:
Bought this based on previous Zen ownership. Nice package but it fails at something very important to me. It does not work with Napster To Go subscription like the rest of the Zen lineup. I contacted Creative regarding this issue and was given nonsensical answers by their apparently severely limited tech support group.
It's an ok player but based on my disappointing interaction with Creative, I would seek a company with a stronger tech support team for any future player purchases.
