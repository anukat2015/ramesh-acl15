Title: Hot air you can be happy about.
Content:
This vent extender redirects into the room air which would otherwise be trapped under my entertainment console.  It's design is ultra simple.  It merely rests on the heat vent and does not attach to it.  If this product were intended for use in a location where the extender could be jossled, then there might be cause for concern.
