Title: Great Product
Content:
Lasko Ceramic Tower Heater with digital display and remote control is a great product. It is easy to set the desired temperature and the heater automatically maintains it. By design, automatic settings are on high heat. It would be better if it can also be set at low heat level.
Would recommend product.
