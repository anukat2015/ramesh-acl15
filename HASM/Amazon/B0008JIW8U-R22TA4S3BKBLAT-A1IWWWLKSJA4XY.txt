Title: coffee for one, maybe two
Content:
This little coffeemaker is perfect for me, since I live alone and drink only 2 cups of coffee in the morning. It makes just the right amount, so I don't have to waste coffee by making too much in the large size pots. Coffee is ready quickly and easily. Brews a great cup of coffee or two. Does not take up much space either.
