Title: Satellite L515-S4960 review
Content:
This laptop is cute, perfect size on my lap. It's not heavy at all, which is a plus since it states that it's 5 lbs. Processor runs fairly smooth, fast. It does NOT have a webcam, I don't know why every website I searched up about this model says that it does. I got this for $100 less at Office Depot. Overall, this laptop is great for everyday use.
