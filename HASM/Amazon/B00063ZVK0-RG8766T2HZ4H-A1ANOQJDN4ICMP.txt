Title: Impressed . . .
Content:
I was pleasantly surprised by the ability of this little unit to quickly heat a whole room (12' x 15'). It is quiet and efficient and beats any little space heater with glowing red, toaster-like coils. These do not glow and are safely buried in the unit so there is no danger of little ones getting hurt or accidentally setting something on fire. Highly recommended.
