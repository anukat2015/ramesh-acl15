Title: Size of laptop n71jq-x1 is misleading
Content:
Just got my new laptop (n71jq-x1) lastnight, immediately noticed size of laptop is not what advertisement displays.
Advertised dimensions are 18.4 x  12.4 x 1.8 .  Actual dimensions are 16.5 x  11.25 x 1.7  
The screen size is accurate, but the laptop dimensions are much smaller than advertised specifications. The weight of 
laptop is also incorrect.  Adveritsed 9.3lbs actual wt. less than 8lbs.
