Title: Terrible computer
Content:
This computer never worked properly. The screen has a problem of blanking when you moved it at all. I had a hard drive failure which I replaced at my own expense. I had to restore and lost data (and time)...then the system failed again so I gave up.
I will never purchase another Asus computer again. Save yourself the money and headache!
