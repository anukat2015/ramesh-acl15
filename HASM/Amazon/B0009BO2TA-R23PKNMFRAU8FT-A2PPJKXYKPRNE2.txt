Title: Hoover Canister Vac
Content:
The vacuum is surprisingly powerful. It is much easier to get around and more versatile than the Oreck portable that it replaced. Also less expensive. Shipping was an issue because the shipping company dumps the item off to the US Post Office. It was lost for awhile.
