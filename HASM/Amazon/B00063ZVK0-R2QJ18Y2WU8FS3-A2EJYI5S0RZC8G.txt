Title: Vornado EH1-0028-06 DVH Digital Vortex Heater
Content:
This heater works great.  We use it in the morning when we come downstairs and want to take the chill out of the house.  It does circulate the air, and we probably are using it for a larger area than it calls for, but it still works well.  Works quietly, and the digital readout on the top is convenient because it alternates between room temp and the set temp so you can quickly tell if it is doing the job.
