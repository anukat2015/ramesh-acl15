Title: Love my Ipod Nano
Content:
The Ipod Nano 16 GB was by far the best value from Amazon! My main reason for buying an Ipod is to listen to audio books and looks like I will be able to load quite a few! The sleek design makes it great to tuck away in a pocket while listening. Audio and video quality is great, I would definitely recommend.
