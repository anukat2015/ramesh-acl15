Title: Lasko 751320 Ceramic Tower Heater
Content:
Very nice product. Works excellently in my room. The temperature control works well. The temperature increments are 5F from 60-85F. It would have been nicer to have some finer controls, but one cannot beat the product for the price it comes it. I will definitely recommend it.
