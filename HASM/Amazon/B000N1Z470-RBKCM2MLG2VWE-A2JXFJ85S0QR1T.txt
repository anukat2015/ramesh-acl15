Title: Instant Heat
Content:
Bought this for my dad to use as a back up for when his heat pump heater freezes. According to him it was easy to assemble and use. He turned it on and within moments it heats up. Loves the remote which controls the temperature, can also use it to set the timer so the unit can shut it self off.  Great Item.
