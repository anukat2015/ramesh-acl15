Title: Makes great coffee
Content:
This is a very good coffeemaker.  I have been using Braun coffeemakers for a few years now and now own 2. Moved the second one to the motorhome. The filters are expensive but worth the cost as friends coffeemakers just don't make as good tasting coffee.
