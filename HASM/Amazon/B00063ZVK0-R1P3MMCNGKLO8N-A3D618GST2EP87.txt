Title: We Love This Heater!
Content:
The price of propane is getting so high, I had to do something...So, taking a chance, I bought two of the Vornado heaters.  They heat our entire house so well that we have to keep both of them on low!  I wish I would have bought them years ago. Now I use the propane for a backup, and it never kicks on.
