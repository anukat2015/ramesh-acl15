Title: The best footrest ever.
Content:
This heater makes coming home from work and putting my feet under the desk a blessing.  Instead of slowly coaxing warmth back into my tired toes, I get an air massage and can really relax while catching up on email and news.  For five stars, it should have a remote control so it could be turned off and on without scrabbling around in the dark under the desk!
