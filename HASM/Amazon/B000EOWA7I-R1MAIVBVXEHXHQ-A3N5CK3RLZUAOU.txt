Title: Good for Car Use
Content:
Many have complained that car vacs this size don't have much suction. I wonder if they think it is to be as powerful as the vacuums at the car wash. This vacuum won't "clean" the inside of the car like a heavy duty vacuum, but it gets the basic dirt up. It's small and convenient.
