Content:
I bought this unit but had trouble with overflow. It may be a simple fix but regretfully there was NO ONE to talk too. The representative would not return calls. I do not have time to clean up a mess in the mornings so this unit is being relegated to the trash.  It may be a fine coffeemaker but it is an even better mess-maker with hot coffee and grinds on the countertop.
