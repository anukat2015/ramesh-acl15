Title: Bunn Preofessional 10c Brewer
Content:
This is an outstanding product. While it doesn't have any of the high-tech glitz of many coffee makers,it makes excellent coffee quickly, negating the need for a timer, is trouble-free and reliable. Would recommend it highly.
