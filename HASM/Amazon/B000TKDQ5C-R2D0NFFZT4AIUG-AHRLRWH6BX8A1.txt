Title: Removes the chill
Content:
I purchased this heater to take the chill off in a bathroom on cooler days.  Simply put, it does what I expected it to do.  It is a straight forward heater with easy instructions and the usual cautions.  Prior to my purchase I had read reviews by others and found them helpful.  I have not been disappointed in this item.
