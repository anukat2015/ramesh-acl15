Title: The Screen and Keyboard are Awesome
Content:
I'm really delighted with this very stylish laptop. It offers excellent performance and a design that is both pleasing to the eyes and ergonomic.  The quality of the display is superb and the keyboard is the best I've ever had, and this is my 4th laptop.  It has the little features I need too, like 3 USB ports so that I no longer have to tote around a hub.
