Title: good for the price
Content:
the 3670G has quite strong suction, good for pet hair. Unit is light and easy to carry. Works very well, but parts seem a little flimsy, wheels seem cheap. Only complaint I really have is that it doesn't have a cord recoil take-up.  Good for the price
