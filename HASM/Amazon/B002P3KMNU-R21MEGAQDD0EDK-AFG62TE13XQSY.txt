Title: Asus is the best
Content:
Hi had to throw out both of my hp laptops because the screen went in less than 1 1/2 years on both.  Now the whole family is on asus laptops and we are very happy with them.  the software is friendly and the keyboard is comfortable except the arrow keys are a little cramped.   asus has a nice web storage package that comes free on some of the laptops that is nice too.
