Title: Try before you buy
Content:
I almost bought this one. Great price for the features, but then I went to bestbuy and played with one. Disappointing, not like the canons and nikons to hold and feel, and live view wasnt attractive anymore.....
The sony dslrs look and feel the best. So goto the storeand try one before you buy.
