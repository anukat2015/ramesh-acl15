Title: Good coffee at home
Content:
We had the same keurig previously and this purchase replaces it. The old one, after lots of use, seemed to be giving out. Since we were very pleased with the Keurig and the concept of Keurig cups, it was a no-brainer to get a new one. Our guests also enjoy having individually brewed coffee with many flavor choices to choose from.
