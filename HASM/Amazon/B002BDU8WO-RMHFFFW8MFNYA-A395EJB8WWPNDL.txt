Title: A good machine, but...
Content:
Technically this is a very good notebook. It replaced an Acer Aspire and I have used it about a month. I like the way it looks and operates. However, had I known about the limitations and expense of Labelflash, I would have purchased a notebook with the same technical features that supports Lightscribe.
