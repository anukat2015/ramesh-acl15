Title: Solid space heater
Content:
Got this for our bedroom with drafty windows.  
Relatively quiet and works well.  
Small footprint.  
Heats up the room fairly quickly.
Looks nice and stylish in a room.
I like the "tip safety" switch - which turns the heater off if tipped over.
