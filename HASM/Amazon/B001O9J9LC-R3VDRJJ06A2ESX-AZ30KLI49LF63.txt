Title: Amazing laptop...
Content:
I just got this laptop last week after doing indepth research of all available laptops in this price range and from different vendors like IBM, DELL, HP etc and I must say that I am glad that I got this one. The screen looks awesome and the bluray movies play like a charm. I am loving every bit of it. No complaints as such on any issues except that the top part of Black color is not as smooth as other vaio models but this is by far one of the best laptops that I have seen in this price range..I will surely recommend to go for it.
