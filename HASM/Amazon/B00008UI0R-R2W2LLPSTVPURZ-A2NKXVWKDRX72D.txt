Title: My Condo Vacuum
Content:
I normally use an upright but needed a canister that I could move up and down the stairs of my small condo that has bare wood floors and some tile.  This was just the right choice, not too heavy but powerful enough to grab all the dust bunnies and grass my little shih tzu helps generate.
