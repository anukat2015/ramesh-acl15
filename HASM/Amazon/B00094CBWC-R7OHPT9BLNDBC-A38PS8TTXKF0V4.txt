Title: Great Heater
Content:
I just purchased this Holmes Heater fan today.  My office is very cold and has cold air blowing all the time.  I put the heater under my desk on high.  For the first time in a week, I'm warm!  You can't hear it running AT ALL.  Can't smell it running at all.  You just feel warmth!  I'm so glad I purchased this one.
