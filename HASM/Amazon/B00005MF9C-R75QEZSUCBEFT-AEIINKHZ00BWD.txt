Title: Works great for me.
Content:
I have one at home and another at work.  I actually do not use the travel mug.  I either put my coffee press in it to fill and when I return the coffee is ready to press and pour, or I put a cup in it with tea bag and again when I return the tea is ready.  Saves me steps in both processes.
