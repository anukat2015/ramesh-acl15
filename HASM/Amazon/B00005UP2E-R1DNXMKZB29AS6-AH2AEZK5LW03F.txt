Title: great little vac
Content:
works great on my hardwoods and is light enough to even carry around to do quick pick-ups and stairs.  I used my first one so much I totally wore it out after several years of constant use.  I was so glad the product was still available.  I bought one for my son and daughter-in-law also and they also love it for hardwoods.
