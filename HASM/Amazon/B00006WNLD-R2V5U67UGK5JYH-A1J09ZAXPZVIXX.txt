Title: Love this Coffeemaker
Content:
I just love this coffee maker. I am a single gal who never drinks more then a cup at a time anyways so it works just great for me. The only downfall is that my "favorite" mug does not fit but I knew that may happen because it was disclosed in the product description. Great deal for the price too!
