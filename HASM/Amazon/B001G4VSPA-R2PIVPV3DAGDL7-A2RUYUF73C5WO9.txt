Title: Keep looking
Content:
I tried this heater thinking that it would work in a room with high ceilings and that it did not.  I had it on high for an hour and there was no temperature change in the room and I didn't even feel much of anything standing next to it.  I am very disappointed in this because of all of the other reviews given on it here and at [...].  If you need to heat a bigger space that has tall ceilings then keep looking because this one does not work.
