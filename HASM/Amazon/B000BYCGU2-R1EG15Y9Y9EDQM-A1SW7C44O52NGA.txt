Title: Great Coffee Maker
Content:
I hesitated to purchase because of the overflow issue. I found this to not be a problem. It has overflowed twice since I purchased it. Once I forgot to put the carafe in them coffee maker at 4:30am, and second my wife forgot to put the lid on the carafe.
This capresso makes great tasting coffee, and is suprisingly easy to clean.
