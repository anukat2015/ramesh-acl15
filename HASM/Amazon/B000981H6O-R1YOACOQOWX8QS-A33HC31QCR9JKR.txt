Title: It doesn't suck..that's the problem
Content:
I bought this thing to vacuum my auto so I wouldn't have to run down to the carwash every time it needed vacuuming. It doesn't have enough power to pick up the dirt on the floorboard or floor mats. So it's pretty useless.
