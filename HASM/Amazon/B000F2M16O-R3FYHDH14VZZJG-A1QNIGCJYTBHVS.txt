Title: Space Age
Content:
I purchased this coffee/espress machine for my husband as a gift.  He loves it.  It is easy to use and the design is awesome.  The only thing he wishes it had is an automatic turn off timer.  His last espress machine had one and he has left it on several times.  Would definitely recommend this product.
