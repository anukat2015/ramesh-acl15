Title: Powerful Little Heater
Content:
When I first received this heater for my newborn, i was stunned to see how small & tiny it was, but don't let the small size fool you. This little heater will heat up your entire room when its major cold, you don't even have to move it up close to you. It doesn't take up to much space so its perfect.
