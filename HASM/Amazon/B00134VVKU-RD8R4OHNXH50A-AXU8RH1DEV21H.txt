Title: The Best
Content:
This is one great MP3 player in the form of a USB drive. Great price, easy install of music, small in size and even the FM radio plays well. A small AAA battery to power it and with easy controls and universal headphone plug. I can't store enough music on its 4 Gig capacity...... This is so cool. I also got a Coby FM wireless transmitter for the car and it plays all this music through the car speakers..... I love it.
