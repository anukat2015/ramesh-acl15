Title: Excellent coffeemaker
Content:
This specific Cuisinart DCC-1200 outshines any coffeemaker that we have ever owned.  If you want really hot, strong, European-style coffee, then test this machine.  Unfortunately, it makes restaurant/friends' coffee seem lacking.
