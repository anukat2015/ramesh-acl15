Title: SeaBreeze SOH7000TA Thermoflo Smart Heat Heater (Don't waste your time buying this product)
Content:
I bought 2 of these to heat up my living room and they didn't work at all. You could feel the heat if you sat right in front of it, but the room didn't heat up at all. I had to wait a month before I got it. It was a waste of time and a huge disappointment. I plan to send them back and get a refund.
