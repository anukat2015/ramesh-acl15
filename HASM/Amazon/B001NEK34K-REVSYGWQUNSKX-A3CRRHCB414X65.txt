Title: Outstanding Laptop!
Content:
This is my second Toshiba laptop.  This first one is running great but we just needed a second and Toshiba was the logical choice. This laptop just humms right along with any hiccups.  The screen is large and clean, the keyboard is just right, and the ports are in the right location.  For the price, I would highly recommend this laptop.
