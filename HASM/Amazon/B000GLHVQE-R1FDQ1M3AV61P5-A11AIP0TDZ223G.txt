Title: Love Our Electralog!
Content:
We first saw one of these in Mendocino and fell in love with its convenience and warmth.  We came home and ordered one for oursevles.  It's easily portable, doesn't get hot to the touch, warms you up wonderfully, and creates a great ambiance.  You do need to be close to it, at least in the beginning, but as you warm up, you'll find yourself sliding it farther back.  We love it so much, we bought one for our son's home.  Now he says he love his Electralog!
