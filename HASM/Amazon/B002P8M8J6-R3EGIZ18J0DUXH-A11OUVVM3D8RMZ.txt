Title: Best upgrade to my previous Toshiba Laptop!!!
Content:
I bought this machine for my sister, who needed a laptop badly.  I choose the Toshiba  brand because I currently own one for a few years and I don't have problems.  First and foremost the specs on this machine and the price attracted my attention. Then when I received the product from Amazon, whose delivery service is out of this world, I couldn't believe how light the machine was for the size and the keyboard is just muaaaaaaahhhhhhh!!! Makes me want to type a thousand words per minute.  Having the full number keyboard at the side like a calculator was also great.  Great machine for the price!!!!!!!!!!!
