Title: Neat heater
Content:
I bought this heater for supplemental heat when I use my basement as a darkroom.  Obviously nothing that glowed would be suitable.  I really like this heater.  It provides even heat and the thermostat works correctly.  It is also silent.  Couldn't ask for more for the price.
