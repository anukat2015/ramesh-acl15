Title: Pricey, but Dependable
Content:
I own two of these, one for my office and one for the bedroom.  Might buy a third one for the living room, since the oil-filled heater I bought is an energy hog and doesn't heat very well.  The energy that this Vornado uses compared to the heat it produces is a very good ratio.  I recommend this.
