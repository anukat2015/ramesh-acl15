Title: Strong second choice
Content:
This great little coffee maker is just slightly second as my favorite behind the Brew 'N Go. When I don't want a full 15 oz cup of coffee, I'll use this little jewel. It's so fast! Can you tell I might drink a lot of coffee? Ha!
