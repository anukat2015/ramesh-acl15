Title: Will not work with any accessories
Content:
Do not buy this product if you have existing iPod accessories that you would like to continue using.  I have a car charger, wall charger, and speaker dock that I use with 2 older iPods, and none of them work with this new Nano.  This is terrible customer service by Apple.  I will never buy another Apple product.
