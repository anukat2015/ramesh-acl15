Title: Love my Keurig B70!
Content:
I love this coffee machine!  We love picking out different flavored and regular coffee without having to brew a whole pot.  The reservoir is bigger than the other models and it makes the coffee strong even if you are brewing the travel size - the other models do not do this.  I did not buy mine from Amazon but I am sure that the warranty is the same...  My old one started leaking and wasn't working.  I called the place where I had ordered it from and they sent me a new one right away!  Now all of my friends have them.  It is the best!
