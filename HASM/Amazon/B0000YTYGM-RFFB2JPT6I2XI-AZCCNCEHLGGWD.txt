Title: Cuisinart Programmable Automatic brew and serve coffeemaker, Black
Content:
The coffeemaker works great and makes a great cup of coffee. It beeps when the coffee is done. Just be careful when you are cleaning it. If you push the auto button then it turns it off. Just make sure the auto button is on if you make it the night before or it won't turn on in the morning.
