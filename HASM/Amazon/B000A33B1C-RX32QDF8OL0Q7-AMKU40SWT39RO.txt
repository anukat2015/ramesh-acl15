Title: no heat here
Content:
After consumer reports gave a great review for this heater, we bought it. After turning it on, we waited 90 minutes for the room to reach the temperature of the rest of the house. And then, after opening the door to the room, it stopped heating and the temperature began to lower.We had to close the door again and wait another 30 minutes to again reach normal.
