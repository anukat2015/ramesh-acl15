Title: Leaves something to be desired
Content:
Although the heater is extremely quiet and heats well...it keeps reseting the desired temp on its own every three of four days.  Ive contacted Vornado twice and have yet to get a response.  Works fantastic if you dont mind having to continuously reset the temp. Think Ive got a lemon that I cant get them to respond to.  A for the heater.....F for their warranty and service
