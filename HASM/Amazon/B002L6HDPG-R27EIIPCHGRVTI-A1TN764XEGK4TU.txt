Title: Apple Does it Again
Content:
The 5th gen ipod is fantastic.  It is simple to use and so compact and light that it is almost as though you aren't carrying anything at all.  The built-in radio is a bonus.  I ordered this for a Christmas present.  It arrived in plenty of time and continues to be a hit with my wife.
