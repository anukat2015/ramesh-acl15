Title: Keurig B60,,,
Content:
It has been a while but I believe the shipper was Handy House. They provided VERY fast shipping and I would deal with them again.
About the B60 unit,it does everything it states. It is great for a person who does not want to make a big pot of coffee every time.
Only complaint would be the "10 second" pump cycle as is a bit noisy. The unit is quick and fast.
