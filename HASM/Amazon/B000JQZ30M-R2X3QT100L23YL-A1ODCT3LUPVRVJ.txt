Title: Lasko 6410 Designer Series Oscillating Ceramic Heater
Content:
The heater is small but works very well to provide supplemental heat in an average size room. Several times this past winter there were cool spots in the lower level of our home and this heater provided just the right amount of added heating to make the room comfortable.
