Title: Great Space Heater
Content:
I purchased this for my office, sometimes you just need to get warm fast and if that is what you want, this is the right heater for you. It has a timer, oscillating feature and auto temperature. It will shut off the minute the temperature is reached. It has a great streamlined look and the price is very good too. Check this one out if you need a good heater.
