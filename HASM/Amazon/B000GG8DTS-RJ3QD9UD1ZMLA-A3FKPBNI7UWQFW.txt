Title: Great little heater!
Content:
It is completely silent! Great little under the desk heater to keep feet and toes warm in a chilly office. Just remember to tun it off, this is a fairly low tech heater and has no auto shutoff of any kind. If you leave it on, it is a fire hazard. If it gets knocked over somehow, it will most certainly start a fire.
