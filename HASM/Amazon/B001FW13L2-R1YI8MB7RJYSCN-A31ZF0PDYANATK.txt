Title: Looks great but poor performance
Content:
I bought this for my daughter. it worked for 2 days. it was returned and repaired by supplier and then worked for 4 weeks more before the tough screen stopped working- without screen, nothing can be done- no mp3, no mp4, no camera. Great price, yes, and great features but no value if it doesn't work right or at all
