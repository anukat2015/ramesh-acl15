Title: Noisy on Auto
Content:
I bought this heater for our daughter's room and like other reviews the Auto is too loud.  I can hear it two doors down in our bedroom when it kicks on.  As for keeping the room heated, it does a good job but I'll be returning it due to how loud it is.
