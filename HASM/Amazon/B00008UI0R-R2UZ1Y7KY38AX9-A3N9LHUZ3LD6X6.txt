Title: LOVE IT!
Content:
Love this vacuum.  This is the first cannister vacuum that I have owned.  I wasn't leaning towards getting the cannister style, but after reading many reviews decided that this was the route go because we have mostly hardwood floors with some carpeting.  SO glad I did - this vacuum works wonderfully on both floor types.  Couldn't be happier!
