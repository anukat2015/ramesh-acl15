Title: This is great for one person
Content:
This coffee maker is quick and accurate. Its awesome for one persone to get one cup and you can use any coffee available. You don't have to wait for a whole pot to brew. The K cup coffee maker cost a lot more and this one is almost as simple. I use this one at work and I love it.
