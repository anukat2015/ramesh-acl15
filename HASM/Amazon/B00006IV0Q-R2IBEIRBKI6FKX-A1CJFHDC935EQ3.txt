Title: I love my Presto percolator!
Content:
This Presto percolator makes the BEST coffee - much better than the most popular (and expensive!) drip coffee makers.  The coffee still tastes fresh even after sitting in the heated pot for a couple of hours, it doesn't taste burnt.
