Title: Excellent Coffeemaker
Content:
The Cuisinart DCC-1200 12-Cup Brew Central Coffeemaker is the best coffeemaker I've ever purchased and I've been buying coffee pots for almost 50 years.  I just purchased my second Cuisinart Coffeemaker; they last forever and the coffee brewing never slows down.  You get a full pot of coffee within 5 minutes.  Excellent product!
