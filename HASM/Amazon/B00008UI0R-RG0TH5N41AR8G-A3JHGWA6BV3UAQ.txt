Title: Hoover S3765 WindTunnel Electronic Bagless Canister Vacuum
Content:
I have a medium haired cat who produces a lot of shedding hair. So far this Hoover produces enough cleaning power to pick up the hair from everything.  I only wish the power brush was electrically powered instead of being vacuum powered.  The unit works great.
