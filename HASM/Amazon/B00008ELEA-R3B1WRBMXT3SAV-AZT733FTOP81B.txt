Title: Good coffee maker
Content:
This is a great little coffee maker, perfect for a person who just wants a cup or two before heading off to work. It is fast and produces very hot coffee. My only compaint would be the spout can be a little messy if you are in a hurry to pour. Otherwise, I am very happy with it.
