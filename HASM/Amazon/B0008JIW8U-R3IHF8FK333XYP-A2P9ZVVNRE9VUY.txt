Title: Wonderful Product
Content:
I love this thing. It doesn't take up nearly as much room as the old 12-Cup product I had before. It makes two big cups of coffee, and it does it on a timer. I wake up now to a giant cup of steaming coffee. I've had no problems with it and would recommend it in a heartbeat.
