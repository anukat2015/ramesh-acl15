Title: Mr. Heater in a pinch
Content:
My Mr. Heater MH9B Portable Buddy Propane Heater worked flawlessly in a recent ice storm. In 9 degree above zero weather Mr. Heater Portable Buddy warmed my Park Model trailer until power could be restored. The quality and ease of use of this product proved just the right item in an emergency.
