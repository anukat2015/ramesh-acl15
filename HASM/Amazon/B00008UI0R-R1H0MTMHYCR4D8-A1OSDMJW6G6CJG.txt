Title: Have one,  bought this one for my daughter
Content:
I have had this vacuum myself for several years, and when my daughter renovated her house they had only hard surface floors.  This vacuum works on either hard or soft surface extremely well, but especially on hard surface.  The suction is spectacular, and you really can't beat it for the price.
