Title: Poor Quality
Content:
Like others, mine worked for a little over a year, and now the fan will no longer spin. I try hard to buy quality stuff so that I don't have to throw it out or buy another replacement product, but clearly Vornado has a quality problem. I will never buy another product made by Vornado again. Nice design but clearly a poor selection of internal components.
