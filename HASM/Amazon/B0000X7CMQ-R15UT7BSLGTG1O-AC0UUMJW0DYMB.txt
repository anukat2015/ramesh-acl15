Title: What a great choice!
Content:
I am known for a purchasing a new coffee maker at least once a year, but I keep purchasing the one that is on sale, and have put out a small fortune in the last 30 years in doing so. I decided to take a chance on this one... spending about three years worth of coffee maker budget and I am very happy! The only thing I wish it were a 12 cupper.
