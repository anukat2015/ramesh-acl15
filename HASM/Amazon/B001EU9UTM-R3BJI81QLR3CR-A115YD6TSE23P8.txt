Title: YES - TO HOME BREWED ESPRESSO
Content:
During our first trip to Italy, we discovered espresso - and couldn't wait to get home and have some regular American coffee...what a disappointment! It was bland, light, and could not match the Italian espresso we (unwittingly) learned to love. After several attempts at purchasing the 'right and reasonably priced' espresso machine, Mr. Coffee came through! At the low end of espresso machines, Mr. Coffee unequivically delivers high end espresso. Good choice for anyone.
