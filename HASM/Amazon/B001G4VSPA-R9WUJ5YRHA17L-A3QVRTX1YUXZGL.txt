Title: Bionaire Heater Works Great
Content:
This is a great heater.  It's completely silent, and yet it can heat a whole house in several hours.  It's pleasant looking, has a toggle button to set high or low, and a knob to turn for the level of heating you want.  On the maximum setting, it can practically heat a 1300 square foot house.  It's a great product.
