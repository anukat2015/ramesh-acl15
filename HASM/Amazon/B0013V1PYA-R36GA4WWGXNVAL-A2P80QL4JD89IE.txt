Title: No off switch. Too big.
Content:
So I was pretty disappointed that it has no off switch. You have to unplug it when you are done. Or you buy their $15 adapter. Oh how convenient. Seems like bait and switch to me.
I do like the heat. If I would do it all over again, the SMALLER pad would be more efficient. Apparently I never spread my feet apart that much.
