Title: Convenient, Great Tasting Coffee
Content:
I have both the Senseo and the Melitta, and I am very satisfied with both machines.  I have a very busy schedule and being able to quickly brew a cup of coffee is a great convenience.  The Buzzworthy is by far my favorite coffee from their selection.  These single-serve coffee machines are great for the home or office.
