Title: good but not enough
Content:
this laptop is beautiful, it works fine but the battery life is very short (around 2 hours with brightness). be careful with the windows 64 bit, is not compatible with all programs.
the video card doesn't allow you to play the latest games in the best quality.
and it overheates easily.
mac is the solution! for everyone who likes to work with audio or video programs, recording music, etc. this pc works, but not how you expect.
say bye to microsoft.
