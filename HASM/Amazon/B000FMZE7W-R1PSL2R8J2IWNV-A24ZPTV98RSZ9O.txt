Title: Not an Ipod!
Content:
My daughter owns a Zune and absolutely loves it!  She is so happy she did not get an Ipod, which had been the rage among her peers.  The Zune has more style, she says, and an incredible amount of memory!  It's easy to use and hard to put down.
