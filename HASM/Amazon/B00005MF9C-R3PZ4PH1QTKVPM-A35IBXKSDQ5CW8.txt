Title: Great coffee one cup a time
Content:
Hard to find , had two previeously both leak after awhile. It makes a great cup of coffee anytime. I take coffee maker on trips so I can have the coffee my way. If B & D used quailty parts so it would last longer than a year or two it would be great. The long run you save on coffee eletricty, and have a fresh cup of coffee anytime.
