Title: Great heater when it works!
Content:
I loved this little heater.  It did a fantastic job of heating up my whole bedroom and monitoring the temperature.  The only problem is that it broke after about a month of use!  The heating coils will no longer heat up.  Now I am very disappointed..and cold!  Hopefully I just have a defective unit.
