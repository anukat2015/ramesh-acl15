Title: Great hotel room size coffee maker
Content:
I love this little coffee maker! I keep this in my home office to make small pots of coffee for me, especially nice when I am using flavored or premium coffees that I only want to enjoy a cup or two. Additionally, when I stay at hotels, I keep the mini coffee filters supplied with the room and use them in this pot at home. Very easy to clean and does not take up much space.
