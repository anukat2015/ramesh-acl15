Title: It's my second one!
Content:
I have owned an earlier model and like it very much.  In fact, my long time girlfriend enjoys my 'brewed' coffee so much that I decided to gift her with her own Presto Coffeemaker so she can enjoy good tasting coffee at her home.  No need for filters, etc. and we get delicious brewed coffee to boot!
