Title: Dirt Devil 082700 Vision Turbo Canister Vacuum
Content:
I recently purchased this product and I love it! I have wood laminate floors and short carpet in one room. I can use it for the entire house and it is light and easy to use. I also like that it doesn't need a bag. Just dump out the dirt into the trash, brush included for cleaning it! For the price, I feel I got a good deal! p.s. The hose is adjustable so my son can manage well and help out with the chores!
