Title: Organized My Life
Content:
Thought I would use this thing for music and videos when I bought this release day; boy was I wrong.
I use this iPod touch for EVERYTHING; from organizing my schedule, waking up in the morning, tracking my workout schedule, shopping online, playing video games, checking my email, and more.
I do not worship Apple but I know when they've got the best product on the market, and this is it.
