Title: Zune 2
Content:
This is without a doubt the best MP3 player I've ever used and I've had quite a few. My son has an IPOD and loves the Zune 2 just as much. 
I've had some regrets with the downloadable software player that Microsoft has provided, but I'm extreemly happy with the player overall. The only reason I didn't give it 5 stars is the software. Too many error codes associated with its use that Microsoft needs to fix.
