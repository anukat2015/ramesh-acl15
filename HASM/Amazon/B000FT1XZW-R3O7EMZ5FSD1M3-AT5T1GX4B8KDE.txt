Title: Quiet and odorless heater
Content:
This heater is pretty good.  It doesn't make any noise, so it's much nicer than those fan heaters in that respect.  It also didn't produce any unpleasant odors, as some people warned it might.  It doesn't produce quite as much heat as we'd hoped, but I think we'll keep it.
