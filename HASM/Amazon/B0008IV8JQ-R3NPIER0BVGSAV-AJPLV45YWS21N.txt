Title: They break - 3 strikes and it's OUT
Content:
I have gone through 3 of these and they have not leaked. But then, none of them has made it to a year. Every one of them has died after a few months... they stop heating up or the heating plate fails. They worked great when they worked. But I am fed up and am now researching a non-Mr. Coffee replacement.
