Title: Great Little Heater
Content:
This is a great little heater.  Compact and quiet, but gives off a lot of heat.  I have 2 larger Optimus heaters, but I wanted 2 smaller ones.  I like the anti-tip switch feature.  The price is very reasonable for what you get.
