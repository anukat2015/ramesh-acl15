Title: Wonderful Vacuum
Content:
A friend of mine recommended this to me to clean animal hair from furniture and she was right!  She has a Golden Retriever and a Ragdoll (long-haired cat) and used her vacuum to successfully remove their hair from her furniture.  Removing the hair from my 2 short-haired cats was much less of a challenge but equally successful with this vacuum.  I highly recommend this for anyone dealing with animal hair embedded in their upholstery.
