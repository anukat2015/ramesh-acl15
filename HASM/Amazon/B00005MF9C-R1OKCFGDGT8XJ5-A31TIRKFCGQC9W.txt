Title: Well worth the price.
Content:
I've gone thru 2 Kurigs.
This little 1 cupper makes a good, hot, cup of coffee.
A little slower on the pour time, no filters to buy, easy clean.
I could have bought 11 of these for the price of the Kurigs.
