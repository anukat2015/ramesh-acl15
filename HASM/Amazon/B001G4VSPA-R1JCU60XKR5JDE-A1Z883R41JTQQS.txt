Title: Great heater!!
Content:
I love the Bionaire Heaters!  So far, the best space heaters in our house are from Bionaire.  On this model, the heat radiates from one side, while the other can face the wall, a little away from a piece of furniture, etc.  And I don't have to worry about the kids or dogs bumping into it and getting burned, since the outside of the unit does not get that hot to the touch. Might buy another one!
