Title: Buy something that provides some heat
Content:
We purchased a Vornado and it didn't work half as good as a heater we already had that cost 1/2 the price! Sure it had switches and controls but what it didn't have was much heat. For the size I expected it would provide a lot of heat over a wide area but it didn't.
