Title: Happy Man
Content:
I bought this for my boyfriend one Christmas because he always stopped by the local gas station for coffee in the morning. Now he can brew it himself right into the &quot;on the go&quot; container and get to work. It saves him a few extra minutes in the morning and about $7 a week.  The cup is made of steel, so it stays warmer longer.
