Title: Great machine!
Content:
Keurig B40 Elite Gourmet Single-Cup Home-Brewing System
This is a great machine for the price.  I love the variety of coffees! My son has the B60 and this one is just as good! You typically only use one size coffee cup per k-cup anyway so you really don't need the B60 with the 4 cups size option.  Two choices is more than enough.
