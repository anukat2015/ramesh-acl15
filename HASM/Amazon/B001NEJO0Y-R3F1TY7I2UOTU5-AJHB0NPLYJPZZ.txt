Title: Beautiful Laptop, Fast too
Content:
I manage several rental properties and needed computing on the go.  We always try to get the most features for the price and this laptop seemed to meet our needs.  
We thought vista would be slow or problematic but was happy to find that the laptop performs well.  All of our programs loaded without any issues.
One of the nicest things about this laptop is that is look's beautiful.  Whenever I pull it out of it's bag, people are really impressed with it.  This probably won't matter to most people but for an in-person professional, it really helps the image. :-)
