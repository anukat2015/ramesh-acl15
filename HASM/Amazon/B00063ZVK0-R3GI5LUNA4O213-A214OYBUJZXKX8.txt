Title: best ever portable heater
Content:
The Vornado portable heater is, without doubt, a lifesaver. Two (2) of these have kept my 2200 sq.foot house comfortable (with no other heat source except my 2 large dogs) for the last 2 months. That is November/December in northern Maine where the overnight lows have been in the low single digits with the days not much warmer. With wind chills taking it down another 10 degrees without the vornado heaters I could not have lived here and enjoyed the snow as I have.
The Vornado rocks......
