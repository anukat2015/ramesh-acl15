Title: Great Coffee Maker
Content:
This is my second Braun coffee maker. I've been drinking coffee for over 50 years, and this is, without a doubt, one of the best coffee makers on the market. To add to it's superiority, a Brita water filter system is included. The only drawback with this particular model is when you make the maximum 10 cups, there is no way you can pour the first cup without the coffee dripping on the counter - the carafe design needs improvement.
