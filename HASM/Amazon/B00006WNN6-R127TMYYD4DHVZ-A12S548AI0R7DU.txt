Title: Year round comfort!
Content:
I use this at work since it seems to be always freezing in the winter.  Then I also use the fan only in the summer b/c people around me use their heaters even in the summer (in the mornings) so it gets hot at my desk.  It's a heater/fan, so there isn't much to really say about it.
