Title: A great machine
Content:
This coffee brewer is everything I had hoped it would be.  Has completely simplified my morning routine, is quick enough to brew multiple cups for guests with virtually no wait time, and I'm saving $$$ that would be spent on bad deli coffee or whatever I'd end up grabbing on the run, if I had no time to drip or press a cup.  It's attractive, easy and there are usually good deals to be found on the K-cups, as well as variety.  Love it!
