Title: Excellent heater !!!
Content:
If this heater ever goes bad. I will buy another. I have tried at least a half dozen other types and this is the best by far !!! I use this in a 12ft x 16ft enclosed sun room attached to my house and the heater never fails to amaze me. It keeps the room at whatever temperature I want. The vortec action REALLY makes a difference and it's so quiet that you don't even notice it !!! This is an impressive small unit and works very well even when the temps dip beloew freezing here in the northeast.
