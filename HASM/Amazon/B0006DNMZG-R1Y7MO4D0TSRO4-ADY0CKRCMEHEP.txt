Title: Nice and toasty now
Content:
This is a fine little solution to a cheap husband who won't turn on the heater at home.  I just plug in this little space heater in my office and I'm toasty warm in no time!   It heats a 12x12' room quickly.    It's pretty quiet and easy to control.  Good purchase.
