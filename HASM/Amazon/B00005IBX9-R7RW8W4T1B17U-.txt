I purchased this item about 9 months ago and I am already having problems with it.  I noticed recently that the clock will just turn off, which disrupts the auto-brewing.  I wake up in the morning and the coffee has brewed,who knows when, and is already cold.  Recently it has just been turning off on its own even though I have the auto-off set for 2 hours.  I am very disappointed in this product.
