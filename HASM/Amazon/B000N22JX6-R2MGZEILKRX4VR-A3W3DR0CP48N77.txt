Title: I freggin love this thing
Content:
I needed a space heater for my apartment but didnt want something too big, or rather something too ugly. 
This was perfect. Not only does it work well but it perfectly fits my decor. It heats up a room nicely, the oscillation is great (not the best but it does the job) and the remote is a perk for when your too comfy to get up and change the setting.
