Title: TA 100 Model Plastic Tanks Faulty
Content:
This heater tank sprung a leak within the 1st year of ownership.  taking the heater to an "authorized" Reddy heater service center required 3 months for the return and sprung another leak within 8 months.
I am in the process of attempting a repair of the tank on my own now.  My opinion of this device - defective out of the box.
