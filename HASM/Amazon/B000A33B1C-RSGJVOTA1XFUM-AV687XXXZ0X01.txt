Title: Makes the room warm and fuzzy
Content:
This product has several nice features that enable you to use this Radiator either as a back-up heat source or just as a room heater. It's efficient, not to large and the built in timer is relatively simple to use. I have 3 of these units and I feel they were well worth the investment.
