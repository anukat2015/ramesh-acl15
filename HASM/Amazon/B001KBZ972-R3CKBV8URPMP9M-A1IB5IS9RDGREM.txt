Title: Mr. Coffee
Content:
This is one of the best coffee makers I've had in years! It is easy to operate and has a lot of features that I like....Like it will tell you how old your coffee is....It will sound a beeper when the coffee is ready and it will go off by itself if I should forget and leave it on!!
