Title: Awesome camera
Content:
This camera is exceptional for the features.  My wife and I were looking to upgrade to a digital SLR and have been amazed at the difference from our old point and shoot.  There are many features on this camera but you can still set it on full auto for those who don't wish to understand all the options.  This has been a great camera and you can't beat the price for what you get.
