Title: Few Instructions, Not User Friendly
Content:
I bought this since it said it was also a voice recorder...there are virtually no instructions included with this product.  I found it most difficult to figure out. The print on the screen is extremely tiny, I couldn't follow the flow of where it was going. I will give it to someone else who can figure it out, because it is basically worthless to me.
