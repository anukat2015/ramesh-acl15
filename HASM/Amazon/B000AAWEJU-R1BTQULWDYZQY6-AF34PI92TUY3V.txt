Title: The best there is.
Content:
This vacuum is pricey, however once in action there is no match! It is lightweight - really! -, very powerful and moreover silent. It beats every vacuum I've ever owned by a long shot, incl. purchases of less than 3 yrs ago. A true recommendation, as it helps me save a lot of time every week and protects my back when up or down the stairs.
