Title: This Is The Heater
Content:
I'll try and make a long story short. 
My 3 year old son sleeps in a cold part of the house but he refuses to put covers on himself. Instead, he comes to mom and dads room and wakes us up to tell us he's cold. Well, that didn't take me long to figure out we had to do something.
I bought 3 or 4 different heaters and they all burned out because we run them all night every night. This heater is the only one that has kept up for the last two years. It's quiet and it works, what more do you want. Don't buy any other heater!!
