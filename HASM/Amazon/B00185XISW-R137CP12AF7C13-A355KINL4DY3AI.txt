Title: Not so hot!
Content:
I bought two of these heaters to boost the heat in a ground floor room that is heated but always a bit chillier than the rest of my house.  I have forced hot air heat provided by natural gas. These heaters do not make the room warmer.  Perhaps they would make a good towel warmer in the bathroom!
