Title: Great Heater
Content:
I put this heater in my insulated garage. It does an excelent job of heating up the entire 2 car garage. My only complaint is that it did state that you needed an external regulator. It should have came with options to purchase one. I had to steal one off of my old gas grill to get the heater working.
