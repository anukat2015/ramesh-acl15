Title: Great coffee maker
Content:
This is a great coffee maker.  We have two of them since we drink both decaf and regular coffee in the morning.   I got the first one about 2 years ago and we purchased another recently to replace another brand of coffee maker.  
Really like the option to set the temperature of the warming pot.  Makes a great tasting cup of coffee!
