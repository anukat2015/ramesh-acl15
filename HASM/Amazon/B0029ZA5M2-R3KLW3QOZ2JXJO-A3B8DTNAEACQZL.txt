Title: Compaq Presario cq60-410us laptop
Content:
As everyone  else says, it was a good price, I bought this laptop from Office Depot online, they only give you 14 days to return it, and after 18 days of light use..BAM!! the hard drive went!!! I called HP and they were a great help, very good, fast and friendly service , the next day I received a new hard drive in the mail and a set of new recovery cd's. They sent me instructions on  how to  install it myself and it was very easy. The wireless conection is very fast.  I am happy with it (considering...)
