Title: A truly great camera.
Content:
This Canon camera if absolutely wonderful.  There is very little that it can't do, and do very well.  It was a Christmas gift for my husband, who thought he really didn't need a camera, and he has hardly put it down since he got it.  I would recommend it to anyone who would like a little more than the 'credit card' sized cameras.
