Title: Old Reliable
Content:
When I replaced my Braun Coffeemaker with another "brand", I was very disappointed.  I missed my HOT, flavorful cup of coffee that I always received with my Braun.  I ended up giving away my replacement coffeemaker and went purchased another Braun.  As always, a consistently great pot of coffee.
