Title: Toshiba M505D-S4970
Content:
I really love this laptop, aesthetically it is truly beautiful and it meets my needs very well.  But I am disappointed that some of the specifications listed were not present on the laptop.  It did not have a led back lit keyboard and there is no HDMI port, the space is there, but no port.  So far I have had it for two months and I have had no problems with it so far.  I didn't return it, because I live in Jamaica and it would have been too costly to return it.
