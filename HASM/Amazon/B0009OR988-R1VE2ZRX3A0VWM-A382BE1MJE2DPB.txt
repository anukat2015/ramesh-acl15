Title: Good canister vac
Content:
This is a powerful and easy to move canister vac.  However, it is ineffective on carpets without the powerhead attachment which was another $160 (from the manufacturer).  Also, you need to order one of the push button (as opposed to friction fit) tube extenders to use with the power head. 
Simpler all around to just buy the upgraded model.
