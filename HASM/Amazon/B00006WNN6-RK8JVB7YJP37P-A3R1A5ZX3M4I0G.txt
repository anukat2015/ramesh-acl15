Title: For that extra heat...get this
Content:
This product is a must have if you don't have suitable heating. A coworker said that he used this very product to keep his room warm and cozy. I bought two of these to help me during cold weather at the office where the heat is not too great. So far, so good! It is basically noiseless and a great investment. I've kept one at the office and gave the other to a friend. She is still singing praises about it!
