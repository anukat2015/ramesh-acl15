Title: Buzz, buzz, buzzz
Content:
I bought this as a gift for my wife, as I'm not a coffee drinker.  In general, she likes it.  However, somewhere over the last two years, this unit has developed a 60hz buzz that has gotten progressively louder.  I'm to the point that I'm going to replace the unit just because the buzz is filling the kitchen, family room, and dining room.
