Title: Great Cup of Coffee
Content:
We purchased a refurb first that did not last a couple weeks but made a great cup of coffee.  I was hooked so took the refurb back to local store and purchased the same maker but without the carafe.  Missed the carafe so donated the machine to our local school and purchased a new one with the carafe here on Amazon.  Great coffee maker!  Love that first cup in the morning!  Initially, there was a pretty nasty odor like burnt plastic but eventually it went away.
