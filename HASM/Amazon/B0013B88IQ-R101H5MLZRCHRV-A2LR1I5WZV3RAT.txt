Title: Free Yourself from iPod
Content:
Free yourself from the Apple madness with this little gaget.  For less than $100 I have a unit that is just as flexible as an iPod, shows all of the same video in just as crisp a detail and I am not forever tied to the evils of iTunes.  This even comes with noise canceling ear buds that do a solid job of cutting down the backround noise even when they are switched off.  You'll never hear the drone of jet engines on your next flight!!!
