Title: Still running after 15 years
Content:
I have a Lakewood model 792-A purchased in 1992.  Both powerful and durable, this space heater has never failed on me.  It provides generous heat even on the low setting.  While it is loud, so ar most utility heaters.  When will it ever quit?
