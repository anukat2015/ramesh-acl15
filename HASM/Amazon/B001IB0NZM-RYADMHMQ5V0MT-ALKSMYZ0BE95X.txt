Title: A staple in our household!
Content:
We bought this vacuum as a replacement.  We've had a
Shark for at least 10 yrs, and we love its portability,
light weight, and choice of attachments.  It's truly a
staple in our household, but its small size and great
suction make great for cleaning our auto interiors as
well.  A real workhorse!
