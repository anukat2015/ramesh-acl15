Title: Handy little heater.
Content:
Whats not to like, plug it in and feel a trickle of heat coming your way.
Removable screen for cleaning. Simple controls. Two heat settings, hot and very hot. Thermostat works fine. Well made and durable. Great for personal use; not for heating a large space.
