Title: Brew Cone 5 Stars...Travel Mug 1Star
Content:
The Brew Cone is wonderful...mmmm...freshly brewed coffee for every cup.
The Travel Mug is another story all-together. The seal on the lid is tenative at best.  Mostly leakes coffee with every sip.  I won't use the lid anymore.  Otherwise it's a pretty mug.
