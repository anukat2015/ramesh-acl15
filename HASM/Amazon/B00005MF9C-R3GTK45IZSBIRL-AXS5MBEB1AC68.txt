Title: Excellent Coffee
Content:
I have owned this coffee maker for over a year. I always use the reusable filter and the mug that came with it. I love my little coffeemaker. It makes wonderful coffee, and I have recently started making mocha which is yummy. I would definitely buy this coffeemaker again. It was such a good bargain.
