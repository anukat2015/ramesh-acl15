Title: So far, so good
Content:
Chose this model because it got a good rating from Consumer Reports. My first Toshiba. So far, seems like great quality for the price. Easy set-up. Speakers are so-so, but then I didn't buy it for that feature. Screen is bright and beautiful. Love the extended keyboard. High hopes for a good relationship with my new laptop.
