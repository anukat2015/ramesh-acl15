Title: Works as advertised
Content:
I ordered this to heat part of our house that never seems to get warm during the winter.  It arrived on time and was easy to set up.  The unit heats the space very well and does not take up much room.  The thermostat on the unit does a good job of controlling the temperature.  My wife and I are very happy with the purchase.
