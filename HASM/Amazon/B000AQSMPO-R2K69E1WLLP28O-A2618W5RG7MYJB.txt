Title: Love it!
Content:
I received my Keurig coffee maker a few weeks ago and its absolutely amazing! I would recommend it to anyone who likes a good cup of coffee, quick and without any mess or cleanup. My kids love the hot chocolate pods and my stops at Starbucks are very rare now. If your contemplating buying this machine -- I promise, you won't be disappointed.
