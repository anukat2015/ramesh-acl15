I recently purchased this coffee maker and I have no complaints whatsoever. Now, I haven't been able to test its longevity (that is a review for a year or two from now!) but I am very pleased with the easy program-ability, the temperature of the coffee, and the brew speed. It is also pretty easy to clean.
