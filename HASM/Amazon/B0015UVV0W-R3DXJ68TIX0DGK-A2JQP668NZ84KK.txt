Title: Room Heater
Content:
Heats well but my when I turn down the volume on my TV with the TV remote the heater also gets turned down.  It does a good job in heating a very large open room.  I have not noticed a decrease in my heating bill yet but we have had a very cold January so the jury is still out on that.
