Title: Great little heater
Content:
I purchased two of these heaters after receiving a heating bill that was higher than I hoped. We turned down the heat in the house and kept these in the two rooms that we spend the most time in. One of these is an enclosed veranda (about 21 x 10) with no other heat source and this heater is able to keep that room quite comfortable.
