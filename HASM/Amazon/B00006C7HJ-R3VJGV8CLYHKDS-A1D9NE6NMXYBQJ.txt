Title: on/off switch failed eventually
Content:
This heater does exactly what is supposed to do. But eventually the on/off switch failed. Cheap components would be my guess.
I had it repaired locally with a new higher quality switch and it is working again. For the price of the heater, they should stop cutting corners on quality.
