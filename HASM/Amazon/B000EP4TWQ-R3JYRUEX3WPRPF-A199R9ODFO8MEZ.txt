Title: iPod Nano 2nd Generation
Content:
The product itelf seems to be fine. Shipping time was minimal. However the packing of the product was substandard. The box it arrived in was crushed on one corner due to no packing material around the product inside the box. The plastic case was therefore scratched on the outside. A little more care in packing the item might prevent damage in future orders.
