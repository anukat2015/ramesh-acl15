Title: Lacks Cooling
Content:
I know I shouldn't expect much because of the price but this is more like a fan than a cooler.  After adding water and the ice packs, you really don't notice the cooling and it's a bit loud.  Save your money and buy a "real" room air conditioner if you need cooling.
