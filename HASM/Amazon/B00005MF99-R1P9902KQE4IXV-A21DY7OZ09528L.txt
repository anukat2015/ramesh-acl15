Title: Coffeemaker
Content:
This coffeemaker has been a great appliance. Do like the style of caraffe, is easy to clean, and with the top cover being easily removed for washing is an added plus. Can use for one cup or 12 and still get the same great quality cup. Its size is much more counter worthy, not taking up a great deal of space. Highly recommend!
