Title: Sunbeam Hot Shot
Content:
Being a long time user of this product, this is my third purchase over a 20 year period. I love the convenience. However, I don't like the new design. The old model was easier. You have to close the lid on this one first and the heat button is harder to push. Also the height is shorter and it's hard to get taller cups under it.
