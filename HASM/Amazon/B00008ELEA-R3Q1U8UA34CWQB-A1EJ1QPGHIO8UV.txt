Title: Great Coffee
Content:
This coffee pot is just as promised.  It makes great coffee fast and the best is that it keeps it at serving temperature with a burnt taste.  I bought this to use in our motorhome as it takes up little space but bring it in the house to use because the coffee is excellent when made in this pot.
